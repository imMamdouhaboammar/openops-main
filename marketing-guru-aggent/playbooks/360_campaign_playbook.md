# 360 Campaign Playbook

## Purpose
Guide the Marketing Guru Strategist agent to deliver a full-spectrum campaign that bridges brand narrative, channel mix, creative execution, experimentation, and measurement.

## Inputs
- Business objectives (revenue, retention, awareness, expansion)
- Audience intelligence (segments, psychographics, JTBD)
- Constraints (budget, timelines, legal/compliance)
- Available assets (brand guide, prior creative, data access)

## Narrative Flow
1. **Context Pulse** – summarize the ask in a single paragraph and surface assumptions.
2. **Strategic Axis** – define the ICP, value proposition, and position against competitors.
3. **Tactical Rhythm** – prescribe campaign waves, hero channels, supporting paid/social/earned programs, and experiment triggers.
4. **Creative Brief** – specify hook, voice, CTA, formats, and mandatory brand elements.
5. **Measurement Story** – assign lead and lag KPIs, instrumentation owners, dashboards, and reporting cadence.
6. **Risk Register** – list at least three execution risks (channels, creative, data) and mitigations.

## Timeline Template
| Phase | Duration | Output | Owner |
|-------|----------|--------|-------|
| Discover | 2 days | Context dossier, risk register | Marketing Guru Strategist |
| Strategy | 3 days | Positioning memo, messaging hierarchy | Strategist & Brand Lead |
| Activation Design | 4 days | Channel plan, budget plan, experiment backlog | Campaign Lead |
| Measurement | 1 day | KPI stack, dashboard blueprint | Measurement Analyst |
| Handoff | 1 day | Launch playbook, briefing packet | Strategist |

## Handoff Checklist
- [ ] Executive summary with KPI call-outs
- [ ] Channel & creative briefs tagged by owner
- [ ] Experiment briefs with hypotheses and success criteria
- [ ] Measurement plan with dashboards or data sources
- [ ] Risk mitigation notes and escalation path

Use this playbook when the request demands a unified 360-degree path from insight to activation. Respect brand, measurement, and compliance guardrails, and always align the plan to measurable outcomes.
