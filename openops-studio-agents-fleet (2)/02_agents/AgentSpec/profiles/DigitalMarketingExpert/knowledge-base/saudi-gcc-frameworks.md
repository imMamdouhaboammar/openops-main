## Arabic Content Best Practices

### Dialect Usage Guidelines

**Saudi Dialect** (الهجة السعودية):

- ✅ Use for: TikTok, Snapchat, Instagram Stories, casual brand tone
- ✅ Example: "وش السالفة؟ جربوا منتجنا الجديد!" (What's up? Try our new product!)

**Gulf Arabic** (الهجة الخليجية):

- ✅ Use for: GCC-wide campaigns, regional influencers
- ✅ Example: "شلونكم؟ عندنا عرض خاص لكم!" (How are you? We have a special offer!)

**Modern Standard Arabic** (MSA):

- ✅ Use for: Government, press releases, LinkedIn, formal communications
- ✅ Example: "يسرنا أن نعلن عن إطلاق منتجنا الجديد" (We are pleased to announce our new product)

## Regulatory Landscape (2025 Updates)

### Ministry of Media

- **Influencer licensing**: Mandatory for monetized creators (500 SAR annual fee)
- **Content disclosure**: #ad, #sponsored, #إعلان must be visible
- **Prohibited content**: Alcohol, gambling, religious insensitivity, political statements

### CITC (Communications & IT)

- **Digital advertising standards**: No misleading claims, clear pricing
- **E-commerce**: Return policy, warranty, customer service requirements

### Consumer Protection Law

- **VAT**: 15% must be included in displayed prices
- **Refunds**: 7-day return policy for online purchases (unless perishable)
- **Data privacy**: PDPL (Personal Data Protection Law) - GDPR-equivalent

## Case Studies (Saudi Success)

### 1. Careem (Ride-Hailing)

- **Tactic**: Ramadan campaign ("Careem and Chill" - iftar deliveries)
- **Result**: 40% increase in orders during Ramadan
- **Learning**: Cultural timing + relevant service = high conversion

### 2. Jarir Bookstore (Electronics Retail)

- **Tactic**: Back-to-school micro-influencer campaign (teachers, parents)
- **Result**: 25% increase in school supplies sales
- **Learning**: Nano/micro influencers drive authentic engagement

### 3. Almarai (F&B Brand)

- **Tactic**: Arabic-first Instagram Reels (Saudi dialect recipes)
- **Result**: 60% higher engagement vs. English content
- **Learning**: Native-language content = trust + relatability
