## Domain Expertise

### 1. Modern SEO (Google 2025)

#### Core Web Vitals (Google Ranking Factors)

- **LCP (Largest Contentful Paint)**: <2.5 seconds (speed)
- **INP (Interaction to Next Paint)**: <200ms (interactivity) [NEW in 2024, replaced FID]
- **CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift)**: <0.1 (visual stability)

#### Technical SEO Essentials

- **Mobile-First Indexing**: Google primarily crawls mobile version
- **HTTPS**: Mandatory (ranking signal + trust)
- **XML Sitemap**: Submit to Google Search Console, update frequently
- **Robots.txt**: Control crawler access
- **Canonical Tags**: Prevent duplicate content issues
- **Structured Data**: Schema.org markup (JSON-LD format)

#### On-Page SEO

- **Title Tags**: 50-60 characters, primary keyword near start
- **Meta Descriptions**: 150-160 characters, compelling CTA
- **H1 Tag**: One per page, includes primary keyword
- **H2-H6 Hierarchy**: Logical structure, keyword variations
- **Internal Linking**: Topic clusters, pillar pages, breadcrumbs
- **Image Optimization**: Alt text, descriptive filenames, WebP/AVIF formats, lazy loading

#### Content Strategy

- **Topic Clusters**: Pillar page (broad topic) + cluster content (specific subtopics)
- **Search Intent**: Informational, navigational, transactional, commercial investigation
- **Content Depth**: Long-form (1,500-3,000 words) for competitive keywords
- **Freshness**: Update content regularly (Google favors recent content)

### 2. Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

#### Featured Snippets (Position Zero)

**Types**:

- **Paragraph Snippet**: 40-60 word direct answer
- **List Snippet**: Numbered list (how-to) or bulleted list (items)
- **Table Snippet**: Comparison tables, pricing, data
- **Video Snippet**: YouTube videos with timestamps

**Optimization Tactics**:

- **Question Format**: Use "What is...", "How to...", "Why does..." in H2/H3
- **Concise Answer**: Place answer in first 100-200 words
- **Clear Structure**: Use `<p>`, `<ol>`, `<ul>`, `<table>` tags
- **Schema Markup**: FAQPage, HowTo, QAPage schemas

#### "People Also Ask" (PAA)

- **Question Research**: Use AlsoAsked.com, AnswerThePublic
- **FAQ Sections**: Create comprehensive FAQ pages
- **Question Clusters**: Group related questions, answer thoroughly
- **Schema**: FAQPage schema signals to Google

#### AI Overviews (Google SGE - Search Generative Experience)

- **Direct Answer Formatting**: Bullet points, numbered steps, clear headings
- **Entity Optimization**: Define key entities clearly (who, what, where, when, why)
- **Source Credibility**: E-E-A-T signals (author bios, citations, backlinks)
- **Conversational Tone**: Natural language, not keyword-stuffed

### 3. Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

#### What is GEO?

**Definition**: Optimizing content to be cited and recommended by generative AI systems (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude) rather than just ranked by traditional search engines.

**Key Difference from SEO**:

- **SEO Goal**: Rank in top 10 → get clicks → traffic
- **GEO Goal**: Be cited in AI answers → brand authority → trust

#### GEO Strategies (2025)

**Content Comprehension & Context:**

- **Entity-Based Content**: Define entities clearly (who, what, where, when, why, how)
- **Topical Authority**: Comprehensive coverage of a topic (become THE source)
- **Semantic Relationships**: Link related concepts, entities, topics

**Structured Content for AI Extraction:**

- **Concise Introductions**: State main point in first 50-100 words
- **Bulleted Lists**: Easy for AI to extract and summarize
- **Numbered Steps**: For how-to content (AI loves step-by-step)
- **FAQ Sections**: Direct questions + direct answers
- **Glossaries**: Define technical terms (AI uses these for definitions)

**Schema Markup (Critical for GEO):**

- **Article Schema**: `@type: Article`, author, datePublished, headline
- **FAQPage Schema**: Question + Answer pairs
- **HowTo Schema**: Step-by-step instructions
- **Organization Schema**: Brand entity definition
- **Review Schema**: Product/service reviews (AI cites reviews)

**Build Citation Authority:**

- **Third-Party Mentions**: Get cited by reputable sources (.edu, .gov, industry publications)
- **Expert Authorship**: Real credentials, LinkedIn profiles, industry recognition
- **Original Research**: Publish studies, data, surveys (AI loves citing data)
- **Content Freshness**: Update regularly (AI prefers recent content)

**Comparison Content:**

- **"X vs Y" Articles**: AI frequently cites comparison content
- **Pros/Cons Lists**: Structured comparisons for decision-making queries
- **"Best of" Lists**: Curated recommendations (AI uses these for recommendations)

#### Platform-Specific GEO

**ChatGPT (GPT-4o):**

- **Conversational Tone**: Natural language, not robotic
- **Long-Form Depth**: Comprehensive answers (ChatGPT favors depth)
- **Code Examples**: For technical content (ChatGPT loves examples)
- **Cite Sources**: Include references, statistics, studies

**Gemini (Google AI):**

- **Entity Optimization**: Gemini understands entities deeply (leverage Google Knowledge Graph)
- **Multimodal**: Include images, charts (Gemini can analyze visuals)
- **Google Ecosystem**: Content indexed by Google = higher Gemini visibility

**Perplexity AI:**

- **Source Credibility**: Perplexity heavily weights source authority
- **Factual Accuracy**: Zero tolerance for misinformation (fact-check rigorously)
- **Citation-Friendly**: Make it easy to cite (clear quotes, attributions, data)

**Claude (Anthropic):**

- **Nuanced Content**: Claude excels at complex, nuanced topics
- **Long-Form Thought Leadership**: In-depth articles, whitepapers, research
- **Ethical Framing**: Claude favors content with ethical considerations

## Arabic SEO Deep Dive

### RTL (Right-to-Left) Optimization

**Website Structure:**

- **Text Direction**: `dir="rtl"` in HTML
- **CSS Adjustments**: `.rtl { direction: rtl; text-align: right; }`
- **Navigation**: Menu on right side (vs. left for LTR)
- **Icons**: Flip directional icons (arrows, chevrons)
- **Forms**: Labels on right, inputs on left

**Typography:**

- **Arabic Fonts**: Use web-safe Arabic fonts (Droid Arabic Naskh, Scheherazade, Amiri)
- **Font Size**: Arabic often requires larger font size than Latin (readability)
- **Line Height**: 1.6-1.8 for Arabic (vs. 1.4-1.6 for English)

### Dialect Strategy

**Modern Standard Arabic (MSA / الفصحى):**

- **Use For**: Formal content, news, education, government, religion
- **SEO**: Official website content, legal pages, press releases
- **Example**: "يسرنا أن نعلن عن..." (We are pleased to announce...)

**Gulf Arabic (الهجة الخليجية):**

- **Use For**: GCC-wide campaigns, social media, casual communication
- **SEO**: Local business listings, social content keywords
- **Example**: "شلونك؟ حياك الله" (How are you? Welcome)

**Saudi Dialect (الهجة السعودية):**

- **Use For**: Saudi-specific content, local events, influencer collaborations
- **SEO**: Geo-targeted keywords, Saudi National Day campaigns
- **Example**: "وش السالفة؟ الأكل زين!" (What's up? The food is great!)

**Egyptian Dialect (الهجة المصرية):**

- **Use For**: Egypt-targeted content, entertainment, comedy
- **SEO**: Egyptian market keywords
- **Example**: "إزيك؟ عامل إيه؟" (How are you? How's it going?)

### Arabic Voice Search Optimization

**Trends:**

- **30%+ of Arabic searches** expected to be voice by 2025
- **Mobile Dominance**: 90% of voice searches on mobile

**Optimization:**

- **Natural Language**: "ما هي أفضل طريقة لأداء العمرة؟" (What is the best way to perform Umrah?)
- **Question-Based Content**: "كيف أ...", "ما هو...", "أين يمكن...", "متى يبدأ..."
- **Long-Tail Arabic Keywords**: 3-7 word phrases
- **Conversational Headings**: Use question format in H2/H3 tags

## Ethical Considerations

### White-Hat SEO Only

- **No Black-Hat Tactics**: Keyword stuffing, cloaking, link schemes, hidden text
- **No Manipulation**: Fake reviews, duplicate content, doorway pages
- **Transparency**: Clear disclosure of sponsored content, affiliate links

### Cultural Sensitivity (Arabic/Islamic Content)

- **Religious Respect**: Accurate Hajj/Umrah information (verified by Islamic scholars)
- **Cultural Appropriateness**: Avoid mixing religious content with inappropriate ads/imagery
- **Ramadan Sensitivity**: Respectful tone during holy month, no disruptive ads during prayer times

### AI Search Ethics

- **Factual Accuracy**: No misinformation to game AI citations
- **Attribution**: Proper citations when referencing others' work
- **User Value**: Prioritize user benefit over AI ranking manipulation
