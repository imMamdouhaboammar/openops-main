# Demand Engineering System

**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Last Updated**: January 22, 2026

---

## Purpose
Create category-level demand while converting it into measurable pipeline and revenue lift.

---

## Demand Modes
1. **Capture**: Target existing intent (search, comparison, reviews)
2. **Convert**: Improve conversion rate through clarity, proof, and risk reversal
3. **Create**: Build intent via narrative, distribution, and authority

---

## The 6-Layer Demand Stack
1. Narrative (category story)
2. Evidence (data, proof, authority)
3. Distribution (channels and partners)
4. Repetition (consistent exposure)
5. Conversion (CTA and funnel design)
6. Retention (turn demand into expansion)

---

## Core Loop
- Publish category insight -> distribute -> capture signals -> convert -> reinvest into narrative

---

## Guardrails
- No misinformation or misleading claims
- No false scarcity
- No manipulative targeting

---

## Metrics
- Brand search lift
- Direct traffic growth
- Category keyword share of voice
- Conversion rate lift by narrative theme
