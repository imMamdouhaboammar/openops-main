# Classified Intelligence Layer (Impossibly Advanced)

**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Last Updated**: January 22, 2026

---

## Purpose
This layer delivers the "unfair advantage" through deep signal intelligence, creative genome modeling, demand engineering, and strategic wargaming. It is designed to outperform any generic marketing agent without violating ethics or privacy.

---

## Core Systems

1. **Sovereign Signal Fusion**
   - Multisource signals with reliability scores and data lineage
   - Confidence-based decisioning (L1-L4)
   - Privacy and consent gating by default

2. **Creative Genome Engine**
   - Extracts and encodes creative DNA (hook, promise, proof, CTA)
   - Recombines winning patterns into testable variants
   - Tracks fatigue and decay curves by channel

3. **Demand Engineering System**
   - Builds category demand through narrative, proof, and distribution
   - Converts demand creation into measurable funnel lift
   - Connects brand trust to performance metrics

4. **Competitive Wargame Protocol**
   - Simulates competitor moves and counter-strategies
   - Pre-allocates budget, creative, and positioning responses
   - Uses risk and confidence thresholds

5. **Moat Compounding Protocol**
   - Converts short-term wins into long-term defensibility
   - Standardizes learning capture and system re-use
   - Protects unit economics and brand trust

6. **Behavioral Archetype Graph**
   - Maps audience archetypes to validated triggers
   - Prevents mismatch between promise and proof

7. **Offer Resonance Index**
   - Scores offer-market fit before scaling
   - Enforces scale/iterate/kill decisions

8. **Attention Yield Curve**
   - Models diminishing returns and creative fatigue
   - Sets marginal ROI and frequency guardrails

9. **Trust Signal Ledger**
   - Tracks proof assets with credibility and recency scores
   - Enforces proof minimums for high-risk promises

10. **Counterfactual Growth Simulator**
    - Models baseline vs incremental impact
    - Requires causal validation for scaling

11. **Dark Pattern Firewall**
    - Rejects manipulative or deceptive tactics by default

---

## Output Promise
Every strategy or campaign using this layer must ship with:
- Causal or incrementality plan
- Signal confidence levels and data sources
- Creative DNA tags and experiment plan
- Competitive response map
- Moat-building actions
