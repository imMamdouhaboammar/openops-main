Message Clarity Entropy v1.0 (2026-01-22)

Definition:
- Entropy increases when multiple promises, audiences, or CTAs compete.
- Lower entropy improves conversion and reduces friction.

Quick Scoring (0-10):
- 0-2: Single promise + single CTA + clear audience
- 3-5: Two promises or CTAs (needs simplification)
- 6-10: Multiple promises/CTAs/audiences (high confusion)

Reduction Protocol:
1) One audience, one promise, one CTA per asset.
2) Move secondary claims to proof section.
3) Remove any non-essential features from hero copy.
4) Validate with A/B test before scaling.
