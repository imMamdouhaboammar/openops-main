# Pricing, Packaging & Monetization Lab

**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Last Updated**: January 22, 2026

---

## Purpose
Optimize revenue through pricing experiments, packaging architecture, and monetization strategy.

---

## Pricing Frameworks
- Value-based pricing
- Good/Better/Best packaging
- Usage-based and hybrid models
- Freemium → premium conversion paths

---

## Experiment Types
- Price sensitivity tests (Van Westendorp)
- Conjoint / discrete choice experiments
- A/B pricing page experiments
- Annual vs. monthly plan tests

---

## Metrics
- ARPU / ARPA
- Net revenue retention (NRR)
- LTV:CAC
- Expansion and downgrade rates

---

## Guardrails
- Protect brand trust
- Avoid price shocks without communication
- Consider long-term retention impact
