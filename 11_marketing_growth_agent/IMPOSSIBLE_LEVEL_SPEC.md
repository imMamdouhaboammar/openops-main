# Impossible Level Upgrade Spec

**Feature**: 11_marketing_growth_agent impossible-level upgrade  
**Date**: January 22, 2026

---

## What
Upgrade the Marketing & Growth Agent to a new "impossible level" by adding causal experimentation, marketing-mix modeling, creative intelligence, pricing & packaging systems, competitive radar, agentic orchestration layers, and an execution-first growth operating mode.

## Why
Current systems are strong but need deeper causal rigor, automation, and intelligence to drive predictable growth, minimize wasted spend, and scale with confidence.

## Outcomes
- Causal-first experimentation and incrementality measurement
- Marketing mix modeling and budget optimization playbooks
- Creative intelligence system for scalable content testing
- Pricing and packaging optimization workflows
- Competitive intelligence radar with automated signals
- Classified intelligence layer (signal fusion, creative genome, wargaming, trust gates)
- Stronger data quality, privacy, and governance standards
- Execution-first growth mode that enforces: 7–14 day validation, explicit first 20 users, exact acquisition surfaces, and behavior-change verification
- Growth idea outputs constrained to the Trigger → Action → Reward → Friction → Metric → Failure Mode format
- MVP (1-day) and MVP++ (1-week) framing for every loop or feature, with explicit “not built” scope
- Collapse and resistance simulation embedded for every strategy

## Constraints
- Must remain fully documented and auditable
- Must avoid manipulative or unethical tactics
- Must keep compatibility with existing OpenOps structure
- Must be readable and usable as standalone documentation
- Must reject recommendations that raise onboarding friction or cognitive load without a short (7–14 day) validation path
- Must disallow abstract claims that lack near-term validation

## Non-Goals
- Implementing live integrations (only specs and system design)
- Replacing existing foundational documents
- Introducing proprietary or closed-source dependencies
