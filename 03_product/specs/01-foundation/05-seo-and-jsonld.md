# Spec 05: SEO and JSON-LD

## Goal

Ensure maximum visibility for search engines and AI agents (AEO).

## Meta Strategy

- **Title Template**: `%s | "$BrandNameArabic$"`
- **Description**: Compelling, keyword-rich snippets.
- **Alternates**: Correct `hreflang` to prevent duplicate content issues.

## JSON-LD Profiles

- **Home**: `Organization`, `WebSite`.
- **Contact**: `LocalBusiness`.
- **Services**: `Service` (with offers and details).
- **Pages**: `BreadcrumbList`.
