# Spec 69: Film & Promotional Video Production Blog Authority System
## Saudi Arabia Educational, SEO, AEO & GEO Optimized Blog (Non-Salesy)

---

## Objective

Create an **authoritative educational blog** about:

Film & Promotional Video Production in Saudi Arabia

The blog must:
- Educate, not sell
- Rank organically
- Be quotable by AI systems
- Build institutional authority for the service indirectly

This content is not a marketing article.
It is a **reference-grade industry guide**.

---

## Core Principle

The blog must answer:
"What does professional film and promotional video production actually mean in the Saudi context?"

Not:
- Why we are the best
- Why you should hire us

---

## Phase 1: Saudi Arabia Landscape Research (Mandatory)

### 1. Market Context Research

Research must cover:
- Saudi media production ecosystem
- Government initiatives (Vision 2030, cultural & media sectors)
- Growth of film commissions, tourism, giga projects
- Demand drivers (events, destinations, brands, ministries)

Output:
- High-level landscape summary
- Key industry shifts unique to Saudi Arabia

---

### 2. Keyword & Cluster Research

Perform **grounded keyword research** focused on:

Primary clusters:
- film production Saudi Arabia
- promotional video production Saudi
- corporate video Saudi Arabia
- government video production Saudi

Secondary clusters:
- video production process Saudi Arabia
- cost factors video production Saudi
- film permits Saudi Arabia
- local vs international production differences

Long-tail / AEO queries:
- How film production works in Saudi Arabia
- What permits are required to film in Saudi
- How promotional videos are produced for government entities
- What makes Saudi production different

---

### 3. SERP & Competitor Content Analysis

Analyze:
- Top ranking Saudi production companies blogs
- Regional agencies content
- International guides ranking locally

Identify:
- Gaps
- Overused sales language
- Missing Saudi-specific operational depth

Define:
- What competitors explain poorly
- What they completely avoid
- Where content feels generic

---

## Phase 2: Content Strategy & Structure

### Content Positioning

Tone:
- Neutral
- Professional
- Educational
- Industry-level

Perspective:
- Insider explanation
- Operational clarity
- Cultural and regulatory awareness

---

#### 1. Introduction

- Explain why the market is unique
- Avoid promotional framing

#### 2. The Saudi Film & Video Production Ecosystem

## Phase 3: SEO, AEO & GEO Optimization

### SEO Requirements

- Clean heading hierarchy
- Semantic keyword placement
- No keyword stuffing
- Internal linking to:
  - Service page (soft reference)
  - Portfolio page (optional)

---

### AEO Optimization

- Clear answers
- Short factual paragraphs
- FAQ schema compatibility
- Neutral explanatory tone

---

### GEO Optimization

- Explicit Saudi Arabia references
- City-level mentions only when relevant
- Regulatory references grounded in reality

---

## Schema & Indexing

Required:
- Article schema
- FAQPage schema

Metadata:
- Informational title
- Descriptive meta description
- No CTA language

---

## Internal Linking Rules

This blog must:
- Link TO the service page (contextual, non-salesy)
- Receive links FROM:
  - Service page
  - Blog pillar page
  - About page

---

## Authority & AI Readiness Rules

- Every claim must be defensible
- Avoid superlatives
- Avoid promises
- Use factual language
- Avoid trend-chasing phrases

---

## Update & Refresh Policy

- Review every 6 months
- Update when:
  - Regulations change
  - Market dynamics shift
  - Major national initiatives launch

---

## Success Criteria

- Indexed within weeks
- Appears in informational SERPs
- Used as reference by AI answers
- Supports service authority indirectly

## Final Rule

If the article reads like a sales pitch,
it has failed this spec.

The goal is trust through clarity, not persuasion.
