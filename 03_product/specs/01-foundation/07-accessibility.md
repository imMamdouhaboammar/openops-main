# Spec 07: Accessibility

## Goal

Meet high standards of inclusivity for all users.

## Standards

- **WCAG**: Target 2.1 AA.
- **Focus**: Visible focus states on all interactive elements.
- **Motion**: Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- **Semantics**: Proper use of HTML5 tags and ARIA labels.
