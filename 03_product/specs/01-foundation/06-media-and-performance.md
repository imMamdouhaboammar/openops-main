# Spec 06: Media and Performance

## Goal

Deliver a media-rich experience without compromising on speed.

## Targets

- **LCP**: < 2.0s
- **CLS**: < 0.1
- **INP**: < 200ms

## Asset Standards

- **Images**: `next/image`, AVIF format, responsive `sizes`.
- **Videos**: Lazy-loaded, poster images required, muted autoplay (no sound).
- **Fonts**: Self-hosted Alexandria and Montserrat.
