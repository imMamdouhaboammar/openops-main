# Spec 65: Authoritative Blog for Event Management in Saudi Arabia
## SEO / AEO / GEO Optimized + AI-Citable Content System

---

## Purpose

Design and produce a **high-authority blog article** focused on  
**Event Management Services in Saudi Arabia**, targeting:

- Government entities (B2G)
- Semi-government bodies
- Large enterprises
- Procurement & communications teams

The article must:
- Rank organically
- Be cited by AI answer engines
- Sound human, not robotic
- Reflect Saudi institutional reality
- Outperform existing competitors in depth, clarity, and authority

---

## Phase 1: Saudi Arabia Landscape Research (Mandatory)

### 1.1 Keyword & Intent Research

Before any writing begins, perform deep research across:

Sources:
- Google Search (AR & EN)
- Google “People Also Ask”
- Google Autocomplete
- Government portals (events, licensing, culture)
- Top-ranking agency blogs
- AI answers (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity)

Deliverables:
- Primary keywords
- Secondary keywords
- Semantic variations
- Procurement-intent phrases
- Question-based queries (AEO)

Examples:
- إدارة الفعاليات في السعودية
- شركات تنظيم المؤتمرات الحكومية
- Saudi event management companies
- تنظيم فعاليات رسمية
- متطلبات تنظيم فعالية حكومية
- كيف تختار شركة إدارة فعاليات في السعودية؟

---

### 1.2 Keyword Clustering

Cluster keywords into:

- Informational (Top of funnel)
- Commercial / Comparative
- Procurement-ready
- Regulatory & compliance-related

Each cluster must map to a blog section.

---

### 1.3 Competitive Content Analysis

Analyze:
- Top 5 ranking Arabic articles
- Top 5 ranking English articles

For each competitor:
- Content depth
- Structure
- Weaknesses
- Missing institutional context
- Missing Saudi-specific nuance
- AI-citation readiness

Explicit goal:
Identify **content gaps** we can own.

---

## Phase 2: Blog Strategy & Positioning

### 2.1 Content Angle

The blog must position "$BrandNameArabic$" as:

- An institutional-grade partner
- Deeply familiar with Saudi event governance
- Focused on execution, not hype
- A reference point, not a sales pitch

Tone:
- Authoritative
- Calm
- Formal Saudi Arabic (for AR)
- Professional institutional English (for EN)

No sales language.

---

### 2.2 Target Length

- Arabic version: 1,800–2,500 words
- English version: 2,000–2,800 words

Depth > fluff.

## Phase 4: SEO / AEO / GEO Optimization Rules

### SEO
- One primary keyword focus
- Secondary keywords naturally distributed
- Optimized headings (H2–H4)
- Clean internal linking to:
  - Event Management service page
  - Portfolio
  - Media Center
- No keyword stuffing

---

### AEO
- Clear answers
- Structured questions
- Neutral tone
- Definitions and explanations
- No marketing fluff

---

### GEO (Saudi Focus)
- Saudi-specific terminology
- Arabic formal language
- References to local context
- Avoid generic MENA phrasing
