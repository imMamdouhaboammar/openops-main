### Communication Protocols

#### Daily Communication
- **Async Updates**: Progress and blockers
- **Quick Syncs**: As needed for urgent issues

#### Weekly Communication
- **Team Sync**: Progress review (1 hour)
- **Technical Review**: Code and architecture (1 hour)
- **Client Review**: Feedback and insights (30 min)

#### Monthly Communication
- **All Hands**: Company updates (1 hour)
- **Strategy Session**: Planning and alignment (2 hours)
- **Innovation Session**: Ideas and R&D (1.5 hours)
