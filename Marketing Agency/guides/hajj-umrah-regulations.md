## 🎫 Licensing System

### Service Provider Categories

#### Category A: Hajj Service Providers (مؤسسات الحج)

**License Type**: Hajj Company License  
**Issued By**: Ministry of Hajj and Umrah

**Eligibility Requirements**:
- Saudi-registered company
- Minimum capital: SAR 5 million
- Physical office in Saudi Arabia
- Qualified management team
- Proven financial stability
- Quality systems in place
- Insurance coverage

**Services Allowed**:
- Hajj packages (domestic and international pilgrims)
- Complete Hajj management
- Accommodation in Mina, Arafat, Muzdalifah
- Transportation during Hajj
- Guidance and support services

**License Validity**: Annual renewal required  
**Quota System**: Pilgrim allocation based on performance and capacity

---

#### Category B: Umrah Service Providers (مؤسسات العمرة)

**License Type**: Umrah Company License  
**Issued By**: Ministry of Hajj and Umrah

**Eligibility Requirements**:
- Saudi-registered company
- Minimum capital: SAR 1-3 million (varies by subcategory)
- Physical office in Saudi Arabia
- Trained staff
- Financial guarantees
- Quality compliance

**Services Allowed**:
- Umrah package operations
- Hotel bookings (Makkah & Madinah)
- Transportation services
- Ziyarat (religious site tours)
- Visa facilitation
- Ground services

**License Validity**: Annual or biannual renewal

**Subcategories**:
1. **Full-Service Operators**: Complete Umrah packages
2. **Ground Service Operators**: Local services only
3. **Digital Platform Operators**: Technology-enabled aggregators

---

#### Category C: Hospitality Services (الخدمات الفندقية)

**License Type**: Hotel/Accommodation License  
**Issued By**: Ministry of Tourism (coordinated with Hajj Ministry)

**Hotel Classifications**:
- 5-Star Hotels (Near Haram)
- 4-Star Hotels
- 3-Star Hotels
- Budget Accommodations
- Residential Units

**Special Requirements for Holy Cities**:
- Distance certification from Haram
- Prayer facilities
- Segregated facilities (where applicable)
- Pilgrim-specific services
- Emergency preparedness

---

#### Category D: Transportation Services

**License Type**: Pilgrim Transportation License  
**Issued By**: Ministry of Transport (Hajj coordination)

**Requirements**:
- Modern, air-conditioned fleet
- GPS tracking systems
- Driver training and certification
- Insurance coverage
- Safety compliance
- Regular vehicle inspection

**Service Types**:
- Airport transfers
- Intra-city transportation (Makkah-Madinah)
- Hajj site transportation
- Group tour buses

---

#### Category E: Digital Platforms and Aggregators

**License Type**: Digital Service Provider License  
**Issued By**: Ministry of Hajj and Umrah (Digital Licensing Unit)

**Requirements**:
- Technology infrastructure
- Data security and privacy compliance
- Payment gateway integration
- Customer support systems
- API integration with Nusuk platform
- Transparent pricing algorithms
- Dispute resolution mechanisms

**Services**:
- Online booking platforms
- Package comparison services
- Customer review platforms
- Mobile applications
- Payment processing

## 💰 Financial Regulations

### Pricing Regulations

#### Transparency Requirements

**All service providers must**:
- Display clear, itemized pricing
- Include all taxes and fees
- Disclose cancellation policies
- Provide written contracts
- Avoid hidden charges

#### Price Monitoring

**Ministry Role**:
- Market price monitoring
- Prevention of price manipulation
- Consumer protection
- Seasonal price regulation (especially Hajj)

---

### Financial Guarantees

**Bank Guarantee Requirements**:
- Hajj operators: SAR 5-10 million
- Umrah operators: SAR 1-5 million
- Purpose: Consumer protection and financial stability

**Insurance Requirements**:
- Comprehensive liability insurance
- Pilgrim health and accident coverage
- Property and vehicle insurance
- Professional indemnity insurance

## 👥 Consumer Protection

### Pilgrim Rights

#### Guaranteed Rights

1. **Accurate Information**: Truthful package descriptions
2. **Quality Services**: As advertised and contracted
3. **Refund Rights**: For cancellations per policy
4. **Complaint Mechanism**: Easy access to dispute resolution
5. **Safety and Security**: Protected environment
6. **Privacy**: Data protection and confidentiality

---

### Complaint and Dispute Resolution

#### Complaint Channels

**Ministry Hotline**: 199090 (24/7)  
**Nusuk Platform**: In-app complaint submission  
**Consumer Protection Department**: Formal complaint process

#### Resolution Process

1. **Complaint Submission**: Via multiple channels
2. **Initial Review**: Ministry assessment
3. **Investigation**: Fact-finding and evidence gathering
4. **Mediation**: Attempt at amicable resolution
5. **Ruling**: Decision and penalties (if applicable)
6. **Appeal**: Right to appeal mechanism

**Resolution Timeline**: 30-60 days for most cases

## ⚠️ Penalties and Violations

### Common Violations

1. **Operating Without License**: Severe penalties, business closure
2. **Misleading Advertising**: Fines, public warning
3. **Service Quality Failure**: Fines, quota reduction
4. **Overcharging**: Refunds, fines, license suspension
5. **Data Privacy Breach**: Fines, legal action
6. **Non-Compliance with Standards**: Warning, fines, license review

---

### Penalty Structure

| Violation Level | Penalty Range | Additional Actions |
|-----------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Minor | SAR 10,000 - 50,000 | Warning, corrective action |
| Moderate | SAR 50,000 - 200,000 | Fine, license suspension (30-90 days) |
| Severe | SAR 200,000 - 1,000,000 | License suspension or revocation |
| Criminal | Legal prosecution | Business closure, imprisonment |

## 📋 Licensing Application Process

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Pre-Qualification
- Verify eligibility criteria
- Assess financial capacity
- Prepare required documentation
- Conduct market research

#### Step 2: Company Registration
- Register with Ministry of Commerce
- Obtain Commercial Registration (CR)
- Register with Chamber of Commerce
- Open corporate bank account

#### Step 3: License Application Submission
- Complete online application (via Nusuk or Ministry portal)
- Upload required documents:
  - CR certificate
  - Financial statements
  - Bank guarantee
  - Insurance policies
  - Office lease agreement
  - Management team CVs
  - Business plan
  - Quality assurance plan

#### Step 4: Review and Inspection
- Ministry reviews application
- Site inspection conducted
- Financial assessment
- Background checks

#### Step 5: Approval and Payment
- Receive approval notification
- Pay license fees
- Finalize bank guarantee
- Sign compliance agreement

#### Step 6: License Issuance
- Receive license certificate
- Activate Nusuk integration
- Begin operations

**Typical Timeline**: 2-4 months
