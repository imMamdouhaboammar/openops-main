## 📊 Decision-Making Framework

### Decision Levels

#### Level 1: Strategic Decisions
**Authority**: Founder  
**Examples**: Vision changes, major partnerships, market expansion  
**Process**: Strategic review → Consultation → Decision → Communication

#### Level 2: Tactical Decisions
**Authority**: Product Owner  
**Examples**: Feature prioritization, roadmap adjustments  
**Process**: Analysis → Team input → Decision → Documentation

#### Level 3: Operational Decisions
**Authority**: Technical Lead  
**Examples**: Technical approach, tool selection  
**Process**: Evaluation → Decision → Implementation

#### Level 4: Execution Decisions
**Authority**: Individual Contributors  
**Examples**: Implementation details, code structure  
**Process**: Best practices → Execute → Document

### Escalation Path

```
Execution Issue
    ↓
Technical Lead
    ↓
Product Owner
    ↓
Founder
```

## 🔄 Operating Rhythm

### Daily Operations

**Daily Standup** (Optional, if team grows)
- Duration: 15 minutes
- Focus: Progress, blockers, plans
- Format: Async or sync

### Weekly Rhythm

**Weekly Progress Review**
- Duration: 1 hour
- Participants: Core team
- Agenda:
  - Progress against roadmap
  - Blockers and solutions
  - Upcoming priorities
  - Quality metrics

**Client Feedback Review**
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Focus: Client insights and feedback
- Actions: Feature requests, improvements

### Monthly Rhythm

**Monthly Strategic Review**
- Duration: 2-3 hours
- Participants: Leadership team
- Agenda:
  - Roadmap progress
  - Strategic adjustments
  - Market intelligence
  - Financial performance
  - Resource planning

**Innovation Session**
- Duration: 2 hours
- Focus: New ideas and R&D
- Output: Innovation backlog

### Quarterly Rhythm

**Quarterly Business Review**
- Duration: Half day
- Participants: All stakeholders
- Agenda:
  - Achievement review
  - Market analysis
  - Strategic planning
  - Roadmap adjustments
  - Investment decisions

**Vision Alignment Workshop**
- Duration: Half day
- Focus: Long-term vision
- Output: Updated strategy

### Annual Rhythm

**Annual Strategic Planning**
- Duration: Full day
- Focus: Yearly objectives and roadmap
- Output: Annual plan and budget

## ⚖️ Conflict Resolution

### Resolution Process

1. **Identification**: Recognize conflict
2. **Documentation**: Record details
3. **Discussion**: Open dialogue
4. **Mediation**: Third-party if needed
5. **Resolution**: Agreed solution
6. **Follow-up**: Monitor outcomes

### Escalation Matrix

| Conflict Type | First Level | Second Level | Final Level |

| Technical | Tech Lead | Product Owner | Founder |
| Strategic | Product Owner | Founder | Board (future) |
| Operational | Team Lead | Tech Lead | Product Owner |
