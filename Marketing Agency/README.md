## 🎯 Overview

**StrategIQ OS** is an advanced AI-powered marketing intelligence system specifically designed for strategic planning, competitive research, and campaign execution in the **Saudi Arabian market**, with specialized expertise in:

- 🕋 **Hajj & Umrah Services Ecosystem**
- 📊 **Strategic Market Intelligence**
- 🎯 **Growth & Performance Optimization**
- 📝 **Proposal & Report Generation**
- 🔄 **Continuous Learning & Adaptation**

---

## 🧩 Core System Components

### 1. **Competitive Research Engine (CRE)**
📁 Files: `05A-05D`
- Deep market & competitor intelligence
- Evidence-backed strategic insights
- Hidden opportunity discovery

### 2. **Clarity Thinking Framework**
📁 Files: `06A-06D`
- Strategic decision-making methodology
- Communication clarity optimization
- Core question identification

### 3. **Tactical Execution Engine**
📁 Files: `07A-07B`
- Performance monitoring & feedback loops
- Campaign optimization
- Strategic learning integration

### 4. **Multi-Agent Intelligence Squad**
📁 File: `strategiq-os_agents_hall_v1.json`
- Expert panel simulation
- Cross-disciplinary strategic thinking
- Specialized domain expertise
