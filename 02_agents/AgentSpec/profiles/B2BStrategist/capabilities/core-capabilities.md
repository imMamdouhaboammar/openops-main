# B2B Strategist - Core Capabilities

## 1. ABM Orchestration (Account-Based Marketing)

### ABM Tiers

**1:1 Strategic ABM** (1-10 accounts):

- Fully customized campaigns per account
- Dedicated account teams (sales + marketing + customer success)
- Executive engagement (CEO-to-CEO, VP-to-VP)
- Custom content (white papers, case studies, ROI models)
- **Deal Size**: $1M+ annual contract value

**1:Few ABM Lite** (10-50 accounts):

- Semi-customized campaigns per industry/vertical
- Account clusters (e.g., "Fintech companies 100-500 employees")
- Personalized outreach at scale (dynamic content)
- **Deal Size**: $100K-$1M

**1:Many Programmatic ABM** (50-500 accounts):

- Automated personalization (AI-driven)
- Targeted ads (LinkedIn, Google Display, retargeting)
- Email sequences (triggered by intent signals)
- **Deal Size**: $10K-$100K

## 2. Stakeholder Navigation (6-10 Decision-Makers)

### Decision-Maker Personas

**Economic Buyer (CFO)**: Budget authority, ROI focus  
**Messaging**: "Reduce total cost of ownership by 35% while improving efficiency"  
**Content**: ROI calculators, financial models, payback analysis

**Technical Buyer (CTO)**: Architecture, integration, security  
**Messaging**: "API-first architecture, SOC 2 Type II certified, 99.99% uptime SLA"  
**Content**: Technical docs, architecture diagrams, security audits

**User Buyer (End Users)**: Usability, adoption  
**Messaging**: "30-minute onboarding, intuitive UI, mobile-first design"  
**Content**: Demo videos, free trials, user testimonials

**Gatekeeper (Procurement/Legal)**: Compliance, contracts  
**Messaging**: "Standard MSA, Net 30 payment terms, GDPR/PDPL compliant"  
**Content**: Template contracts, compliance certifications

## 3. Long Sales Cycle Mastery (3-18 Months)

### Stage-by-Stage Tactics

| Stage | Duration | Objective | Key Activities |
|-------|----------|-----------|----------------|
| **Awareness** | 0-1mo | Brand recognition | Thought leadership, ABM ads, LinkedIn content |
| **Consideration** | 1-3mo | Generate interest | Demos, webinars, case studies |
| **Evaluation** | 3-6mo | Prove value | POC/pilot, technical deep-dive, security review |
| **Proposal** | 6-9mo | Commercial alignment | Custom proposal, pricing negotiation |
| **Procurement** | 9-12mo | Legal/contractual | Contract review, redlines, final signatures |

### Deal Acceleration Techniques

- **Champion Development**: Identify + empower internal advocate
- **Executive Sponsorship**: C-level to C-level engagement
- **Mutual Success Plan**: Co-created timeline with milestones
- **Risk Mitigation**: Address objections early (technical, budget, political)

## 4. RFP & Procurement Excellence

### Go/No-Go Decision Framework

**Score each criterion 1-5, proceed only if total ≥ 18/25**:

- Strategic fit with ICP (5 = perfect)
- Relationship strength (5 = strong champion)
- Win probability (5 = ≥70%)
- Team bandwidth (5 = fully available)
- Deal size/margin (5 = high value)

### RFP Response Structure

1. **Executive Summary** (1-2 pages): Problem + solution + value proposition
2. **Understanding Requirements** (5-10 pages): Restate client pain points, show empathy
3. **Proposed Solution** (15-20 pages): Detailed approach, methodology, technology
4. **Value Proposition** (3-5 pages): Quantified outcomes + proof (case studies, references)
5. **Team & Credentials** (5-10 pages): Bios, certifications, relevant experience
6. **Pricing** (2-3 pages): Transparent breakdown, payment terms, optional add-ons
7. **Appendices**: Technical specs, compliance docs, testimonials

## 5. Saudi B2B/B2G Market Navigation

### Government (B2G) Procurement

**Key Platforms**:

- **Etimad**: Saudi government e-procurement platform (tenders, RFQs, auctions)
- **Monaqasat**: Bidding portal for public projects
- **Ministry portals**: Sector-specific procurement (Health, Education, Digital Gov)

**Vision 2030 Alignment** (CRITICAL):

- Digital transformation initiatives
- Saudization targets (% Saudi employees)
- Local content requirements (% local suppliers/manufacturing)
- Sustainability (ESG criteria)

### Private Sector B2B

**Large Corporations** (Aramco, STC, Al Rajhi Bank):

- Formal procurement processes
- RFPs, multi-stage evaluations
- 6-18 month sales cycles

**Family Businesses**:

- Relationship-driven (personal trust before business)
- Patriarch/matriarch approval often required
- Long courtship (dinners, social events, Ramadan gatherings)

**Cultural Best Practices**:

- **Patience**: Don't rush, expect 2-3x longer cycles
- **Respect Hierarchy**: Engage senior executives early
- **Build Trust**: Multiple in-person meetings before discussing price
- **Ramadan/Hajj**: Business slows, plan around holy months
- **Arabic Materials**: Provide Arabic versions of proposals/presentations (not just English)
