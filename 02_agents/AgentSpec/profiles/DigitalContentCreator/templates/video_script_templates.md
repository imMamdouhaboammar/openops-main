# Video Script Templates - Reels & TikTok

## 🎬 Purpose

Ready-to-use templates for creating engaging Reels and TikTok scripts with proven structures.

---

## 📐 Universal Script Structure

### Hook (0-3 seconds)

**Purpose:** Stop the scroll

### Body (3-20 seconds)

**Purpose:** Deliver value/entertainment

### CTA (Last 3 seconds)

**Purpose:** Drive action

---

## 🎯 Template 1: Tutorial/How-To

**Best For:** Educational content, product demos, tips

```
[HOOK - 0-3s]
Visual: Close-up of problem/before state
Text Overlay: "3 Steps to [Desired Result]"
Audio: Trending sound (upbeat)

[BODY - 3-18s]
Scene 1 (5s):
Visual: Step 1 demonstration
Text: "1. [Action]"
Voiceover/Caption: Brief explanation

Scene 2 (5s):
Visual: Step 2 demonstration
Text: "2. [Action]"
Voiceover/Caption: Brief explanation

Scene 3 (5s):
Visual: Step 3 demonstration
Text: "3. [Action]"
Voiceover/Caption: Brief explanation

[CTA - 18-21s]
Visual: Final result/transformation
Text: "Save this for later! ✨"
Audio: Music peak
```

**Example - Skincare:**

```
Hook: "How I fixed my dry skin in 3 steps"
Body:
  1. Gentle cleanser (not harsh)
  2. Hydrating serum
  3. Moisturizer while skin is damp
CTA: "Follow for more skincare tips!"
```

## 🎯 Template 3: Trend Participation

**Best For:** Riding viral trends, staying relevant

```
[HOOK - 0-3s]
Visual: Trend-specific opening
Text: "[Trend phrase] but make it [your niche]"
Audio: Trending sound (MUST use exact trending audio)

[BODY - 3-18s]
Follow trend format but adapt to your brand:
- Use trend structure
- Insert your product/message
- Keep authentic to trend
- Add unique twist

[CTA - 18-21s]
Visual: Brand/product
Text: Trend-appropriate CTA
```

**Example - "Tell me you're X without telling me you're X":**

```
Hook: "Tell me you're obsessed with skincare without telling me"
Body:
  - Shows 10-step routine
  - Fridge full of serums
  - Knows all ingredients by heart
CTA: "Tag a skincare addict!"
```

## 📱 Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Instagram Reels

**Length:** 15-90 seconds (sweet spot: 30-60s)

**Audio:**

- Use trending audio for discovery
- Original audio for brand building
- Mix both strategies

**Text Overlays:**

- Large, readable font
- High contrast
- Key points only
- Sync with audio

**Hashtags:**

- 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Mix popular + niche
- Include in caption, not on video

**Best Practices:**

- Vertical format (9:16)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Keep fast-paced
- End with CTA

---

### TikTok

**Length:** 15-60 seconds (sweet spot: 21-34s)

**Sound:**

- Trending sounds are CRITICAL
- Use exact trending audio
- Don't modify trending sounds
- Original sounds for brand

**Text:**

- Minimal text overlays
- Let visuals speak
- Use captions for accessibility

**Hashtags:**

- 3-5 hashtags
- Include #FYP, #ForYou
- Niche-specific tags

**Best Practices:**

- Authentic, not polished
- Participate in trends
- Duet/Stitch opportunities
- Engage in comments

## ✍️ Script Writing Tips

### Do's

✅ Start with strong hook
✅ Keep it fast-paced
✅ One clear message
✅ Use trending audio
✅ Include text overlays
✅ End with clear CTA
✅ Make it rewatchable

### Don'ts

❌ Slow intros
❌ Too much text
❌ Multiple messages
❌ Ignore trends
❌ No CTA
❌ Too long
❌ Boring visuals

## 💡 Quick Script Formula

**For when you need a script FAST:**

1. **Hook:** "[Attention-grabbing statement]"
2. **Promise:** "Here's how to [solve problem]"
3. **Deliver:** Show 3 quick steps/tips
4. **CTA:** "Save this!" or "Follow for more"

**Time:** 21 seconds total
**Format:** Works for both Reels & TikTok

---

## 🎬 Example: Complete Script

**Product:** Vitamin C Serum
**Goal:** Drive awareness
**Platform:** Instagram Reels
**Length:** 30 seconds

```
[0-3s] HOOK
Visual: Close-up of dull skin
Text: "Why your skin looks dull"
Audio: Trending sound (upbeat)

[3-9s] PROBLEM
Visual: Point to skin
Text: "Missing: Vitamin C"
Voiceover: "Your skin needs vitamin C"

[9-15s] SOLUTION
Visual: Apply serum
Text: "This changed everything"
Voiceover: "Our vitamin C serum"

[15-21s] BENEFITS
Visual: Glowing skin
Text: "Brighter in 2 weeks"
Voiceover: "Visible glow"

[21-24s] PROOF
Visual: Before/after split
Text: "Real results"

[24-30s] CTA
Visual: Product shot
Text: "Link in bio ✨"
Voiceover: "Get yours now"
```
