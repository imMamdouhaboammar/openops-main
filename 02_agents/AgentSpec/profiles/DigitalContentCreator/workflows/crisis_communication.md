# Crisis Communication Protocol

## Rapid Response Framework for Social Media Crises

---

## 🚨 Crisis Definition

**A crisis is:**

- Negative viral content about your brand
- Product/service failure affecting customers
- Controversial statement/action
- Data breach or security issue
- Customer safety concern
- Widespread negative sentiment

**Not a crisis:**

- Single negative comment
- Minor complaint
- Constructive criticism
- Isolated incident

---

## ⚡ Crisis Response Framework

### Level 1: Minor Issue (Green)

**Examples:** Few negative comments, minor complaint

**Response Time:** Within 2 hours
**Action:**

- Monitor situation
- Respond professionally
- Resolve privately if possible
- Document incident

---

### Level 2: Moderate Issue (Yellow)

**Examples:** Multiple complaints, negative post gaining traction

**Response Time:** Within 1 hour
**Action:**

- Activate crisis team
- Prepare holding statement
- Monitor all channels
- Respond publicly
- Escalate if needed

---

### Level 3: Major Crisis (Red)

**Examples:** Viral negative content, widespread outrage, media coverage

**Response Time:** Within 30 minutes
**Action:**

- Full crisis mode
- Leadership involved
- Official statement
- Multi-channel response
- Media monitoring
- Legal review (if needed)

---

## 📋 Crisis Response Steps

### Step 1: Assess (First 15 minutes)

**Questions to Answer:**

1. What happened?
2. How serious is it?
3. Who is affected?
4. Is it spreading?
5. What's the sentiment?
6. Is it factual or misinformation?

**Crisis Level:**

- [ ] Level 1 (Green)
- [ ] Level 2 (Yellow)
- [ ] Level 3 (Red)

---

### Step 2: Assemble Team (Minutes 15-30)

**Crisis Team:**

- Crisis Manager (leads response)
- Social Media Manager (monitors/responds)
- PR/Communications (messaging)
- Legal (if needed)
- Leadership (approval)

**Communication:**

- Internal chat group
- Clear roles assigned
- Decision-maker identified

---

### Step 3: Pause Regular Content (Immediate)

**Actions:**

- [ ] Pause scheduled posts
- [ ] Cancel promotional content
- [ ] Hold ads (if appropriate)
- [ ] Brief team on silence

**Why:** Avoid appearing tone-deaf

---

### Step 4: Gather Facts (Minutes 30-60)

**Information Needed:**

- What actually happened?
- Timeline of events
- Who is affected?
- What's our responsibility?
- What are we doing about it?

**Sources:**

- Internal teams
- Customer service
- Operations
- Legal
- Affected parties

---

### Step 5: Craft Response (Minutes 60-90)

**Response Components:**

**1. Acknowledgment**

```
"We are aware of [issue] and take this very seriously."
```

**2. Empathy**

```
"We understand the concern/frustration this has caused."
```

**3. Action**

```
"We are immediately [action being taken]."
```

**4. Commitment**

```
"We are committed to [resolution/prevention]."
```

**5. Next Steps**

```
"We will update you within [timeframe]."
```

**Example:**

```
نحن على علم بالمشكلة التي حدثت مع [المنتج/الخدمة] ونأخذ هذا الأمر على محمل الجد.

نتفهم تماماً القلق الذي سببه هذا الموقف لعملائنا.

قمنا فوراً بـ [الإجراء المتخذ] ونعمل على [الحل].

نلتزم بـ [الوعد بالتحسين/الحل].

سنقوم بتحديثكم خلال [الإطار الزمني].
```

---

### Step 6: Publish Response (Minutes 90-120)

**Where to Post:**

- [ ] Instagram Story + Feed
- [ ] Facebook Post
- [ ] Twitter/X Thread
- [ ] LinkedIn (if B2B)
- [ ] Website (if major)

**Approval:**

- [ ] Legal reviewed (if needed)
- [ ] Leadership approved
- [ ] Messaging consistent

---

### Step 7: Monitor & Respond (Ongoing)

**Monitor:**

- All social channels
- Brand mentions
- Hashtags
- News coverage
- Competitor activity

**Respond:**

- Acknowledge comments
- Provide updates
- Correct misinformation
- Stay professional
- Don't argue

---

### Step 8: Update Regularly

**Update Frequency:**

- Major crisis: Every 2-4 hours
- Moderate: Every 6-12 hours
- Minor: As needed

**Update Template:**

```
تحديث [الوقت]:

[ما تم إنجازه]
[الوضع الحالي]
[الخطوات القادمة]

شكراً لصبركم وتفهمكم.
```

---

### Step 9: Resolution

**When Crisis Subsides:**

- [ ] Final update posted
- [ ] Thank community
- [ ] Outline preventive measures
- [ ] Resume normal content (gradually)

**Final Statement Template:**

```
شكراً لكم على صبركم وتفهمكم خلال هذه الفترة.

تم حل المشكلة بـ [الحل].

اتخذنا الإجراءات التالية لمنع تكرار ذلك:
- [إجراء 1]
- [إجراء 2]
- [إجراء 3]

نقدر ثقتكم المستمرة فينا.
```

---

### Step 10: Post-Crisis Analysis

**Debrief Questions:**

1. What triggered the crisis?
2. How quickly did we respond?
3. Was our response effective?
4. What could we have done better?
5. How do we prevent this?

**Document:**

- Timeline of events
- Actions taken
- Lessons learned
- Process improvements

---

## 🚫 Crisis Response DON'Ts

### ❌ Never

1. **Delete negative comments** (unless spam/abusive)
2. **Argue with customers**
3. **Make excuses**
4. **Blame others**
5. **Ignore the issue**
6. **Respond emotionally**
7. **Make promises you can't keep**
8. **Post promotional content during crisis**

### ✅ Always

1. **Acknowledge quickly**
2. **Show empathy**
3. **Take responsibility** (if appropriate)
4. **Provide solutions**
5. **Be transparent**
6. **Stay professional**
7. **Follow through**
8. **Learn and improve**

## 📱 Crisis Communication Templates

### Holding Statement (When gathering facts)

```
نحن على علم بالموقف ونقوم حالياً بجمع المعلومات الكاملة.

سنقوم بتحديثكم خلال [الإطار الزمني] بمجرد أن تكون لدينا معلومات أكثر.

شكراً لصبركم.
```

### Apology Statement

```
نعتذر بشدة عن [المشكلة].

هذا لا يعكس معاييرنا ونتحمل المسؤولية الكاملة.

قمنا بـ [الإجراء] ونلتزم بـ [الحل].

نقدر تفهمكم ونعدكم بعدم تكرار ذلك.
```

### Misinformation Correction

```
لقد لاحظنا انتشار معلومات غير صحيحة حول [الموضوع].

الحقائق هي:
- [حقيقة 1]
- [حقيقة 2]
- [حقيقة 3]

نحن ملتزمون بالشفافية ومتاحون للإجابة على أي أسئلة.
```

## ✅ Crisis Prevention

**Proactive Measures:**

1. **Monitor constantly:** Social listening tools
2. **Respond quickly:** Address issues early
3. **Build goodwill:** Strong community relationships
4. **Quality control:** Prevent issues at source
5. **Train team:** Crisis response training
6. **Have plan:** Crisis protocol ready

---

**Remember:** How you handle a crisis can make or break your brand. Respond quickly, empathetically, and professionally! 🚨
