# Integrated Campaign Management

## Multi-Channel Campaign Planning & Execution Framework

---

## 🎯 Campaign Framework

### Campaign Types

**1. Product Launch**

- Timeline: 4-6 weeks
- Goal: Awareness + Sales
- Channels: All platforms

**2. Brand Awareness**

- Timeline: Ongoing
- Goal: Reach + Recognition
- Channels: Focus on reach platforms

**3. Engagement Campaign**

- Timeline: 2-4 weeks
- Goal: Community building
- Channels: Interactive platforms

**4. Conversion Campaign**

- Timeline: 1-2 weeks
- Goal: Sales/Sign-ups
- Channels: High-intent platforms

**5. Seasonal Campaign**

- Timeline: Seasonal (Ramadan, Summer, etc.)
- Goal: Relevance + Sales
- Channels: All platforms

## ✅ Best Practices

1. **Plan ahead:** 4-6 weeks minimum
2. **Stay flexible:** Adapt to trends
3. **Monitor closely:** Daily checks
4. **Engage actively:** Respond quickly
5. **Document learnings:** Build knowledge
6. **Celebrate wins:** Team morale
