# Creative Mode Workflow

## 🎨 When to Use

**Trigger:** User requests creative, viral, comedy, or meme content

**Keywords:**

- "Creative"
- "Viral"
- "Funny"
- "Comedy"
- "Meme"
- "Entertaining"
- "Humorous"
- "Trend-based"

---

## 🔄 Modified Workflow for Creative Content

### Stage 1: Safety & Scope

**Same as standard workflow**

**Additional Creative Checks:**

- Is humor appropriate for brand?
- Are there sensitive topics to avoid?
- What's the creative objective?

---

### Stage 2: Content Discovery

**Focus:** Creative patterns and inspiration

**Activities:**

- Review successful creative content
- Analyze trending formats
- Study competitor creative approaches
- Identify creative opportunities

**Output:** Creative landscape understanding

---

### Stage 3: CQL Lock

**Focus:** Creative objective definition

**Lock Questions:**

- What's the creative goal? (entertain, inspire, engage)
- What emotion should it trigger?
- What's the desired audience reaction?
- How does it support brand objectives?

**Output:** Locked creative objective

---

### Stage 4: Trend Analysis ⭐ NEW

**Focus:** Real-time trend evaluation

**Activities:**

- Check platform trending pages
- Evaluate trend relevance
- Assess timing and risk
- Determine participation strategy

**Decision:** Participate in trend or create original?

**Output:** Trend participation decision

---

### Stage 5: Research & Grounding

**Focus:** Creative patterns + **Fact Verification**

**Creative Research:**

- Review creative frameworks
- Study humor styles
- Analyze viral patterns
- Explore ideation techniques

**Fact Verification:** 🛡️ CRITICAL

- Identify all factual claims
- Verify against evidence sources
- Document sources
- Flag unverified elements

**Output:** Creative approach + verified facts

---

### Stage 6: Creative Ideation ⭐ NEW

**Focus:** Generate creative concepts

**Process:**

1. **Diverge** (10 min)
   - Generate multiple ideas
   - No filtering
   - Quantity over quality

2. **Converge** (10 min)
   - Group similar ideas
   - Identify standouts
   - Eliminate weak ideas

3. **Evaluate** (10 min)
   - Score top ideas
   - Use idea evaluation matrix
   - Select winner

**Output:** Selected creative concept

---

### Stage 7: Content Strategy

**Focus:** Creative execution plan

**Strategy Elements:**

- Creative framework selection
- Humor style (if applicable)
- Format choice
- Platform optimization
- Timing strategy

**Output:** Creative execution strategy

---

### Stage 8: Content Creation

**Focus:** Creative execution

**Creation Process:**

- Apply creative frameworks
- Use humor techniques (if applicable)
- Maintain brand voice
- Ensure cultural appropriateness
- Keep factual elements separate

**Output:** Draft creative content

---

### Stage 9: Fact-Checking Gate ⭐ NEW

**Focus:** Verify all factual claims

**Process:**

1. Extract all claims
2. Categorize (creative vs factual)
3. Verify factual claims
4. Flag unverified
5. Resolve or remove

**Blocking:** Cannot proceed if fact-checking fails

**Output:** Fact-checked content

---

### Stage 10: Quality Assurance

**Focus:** Standard + Creative quality gates

**Standard Gates:**

1. Clarity Gate
2. Brand Alignment Gate
3. Audience Relevance Gate
4. Platform Optimization Gate
5. Cultural Appropriateness Gate
6. Evidence Gate (fact-checking)

**Creative Gates:** ⭐ NEW
7. Originality Gate (≥7/10)
8. Impact Gate (≥8/10)
9. Execution Gate (≥8/10)
10. Cultural Fit Gate (≥9/10)

**Overall Creative Score:** ≥7.5/10 to proceed

**Output:** Quality-assured creative content

---

### Stage 11: Delivery & Documentation

**Same as standard workflow**

**Additional Documentation:**

- Creative approach used
- Trend participation (if any)
- Creative scores
- Audience response (post-publish)

---

## 🚨 Critical Safeguards

### Anti-Hallucination Checkpoints

**Before Creation:**

- ✅ All brand info verified
- ✅ Product claims documented
- ✅ Statistics sourced

**During Creation:**

- ✅ Creative vs factual separated
- ✅ Exaggeration obviously exaggerated
- ✅ No forbidden fabrications

**After Creation:**

- ✅ Fact-checking gate passed
- ✅ All claims verified
- ✅ Sources documented

## 🎨 Example: Comedy Caption Creation

**Request:** "Create funny caption for skincare post"

**Stage 1-3:** Safety, Discovery, CQL Lock

- Objective: Entertain while educating
- Emotion: Laughter + relatability
- Brand: Maintain professional yet fun voice

**Stage 4:** Trend Analysis

- Check current memes
- No specific trend to join
- Create original humor

**Stage 5:** Research

- Review Egyptian humor styles
- Select: Self-deprecating + observational
- Verify: No factual claims needed

**Stage 6:** Ideation

- Generate 5 funny concepts
- Select: "Skincare routine reality vs expectation"

**Stage 7:** Strategy

- Format: Comparison humor
- Style: Relatable self-deprecation
- Platform: Instagram

**Stage 8:** Creation

```
Caption:
"My skincare routine in my head: 10 steps, glowing skin, organized queen 👑

My actual routine: Forgot to wash face, fell asleep with makeup, using yesterday's moisturizer 😅

Who else? 🙋‍♀️"
```

**Stage 9:** Fact-Checking

- No factual claims
- Pure creative/relatable content
- ✅ Pass

**Stage 10:** Quality Gates

- Standard gates: ✅ Pass
- Creative gates:
  - Originality: 7/10
  - Impact: 8/10 (relatable humor)
  - Execution: 8/10
  - Cultural Fit: 9/10
- Overall: 8.0/10 ✅ Pass

**Stage 11:** Deliver
