## Customer Journey Mapping

### Journey Stages

**1. Awareness**

- **Mindset**: Problem-aware or solution-unaware
- **Questions**: "What's wrong?" "Is there a solution?"
- **Content Needs**: Educational, problem-identification
- **Emotions**: Confused, frustrated, curious
- **Touchpoints**: Social media, Search, Word-of-mouth

**2. Consideration**

- **Mindset**: Solution-aware, evaluating options
- **Questions**: "Which solution is best?" "What are my options?"
- **Content Needs**: Comparison, features, benefits
- **Emotions**: Hopeful, analytical, cautious
- **Touchpoints**: Website, Reviews, Comparisons

**3. Decision**

- **Mindset**: Ready to purchase, final evaluation
- **Questions**: "Is this the right choice?" "Can I trust this?"
- **Content Needs**: Proof, testimonials, guarantees
- **Emotions**: Excited, nervous, decisive
- **Touchpoints**: Product pages, Checkout, Customer service

**4. Retention**

- **Mindset**: Post-purchase, seeking value
- **Questions**: "Did I make the right choice?" "How do I use this?"
- **Content Needs**: Onboarding, support, tips
- **Emotions**: Satisfied, curious, engaged
- **Touchpoints**: Email, Support, Community

**5. Advocacy**

- **Mindset**: Loyal, willing to recommend
- **Questions**: "Who else needs this?" "How can I share?"
- **Content Needs**: Referral programs, UGC opportunities
- **Emotions**: Delighted, proud, enthusiastic
- **Touchpoints**: Social sharing, Reviews, Referrals

## Egyptian Market Research Tips

**Research Approach:**

- Face-to-face interviews often more effective than online
- Family input important in decision-making
- Religious sensitivity in questions
- Gender considerations in mixed groups

**Data Collection:**

- WhatsApp surveys work well
- Phone interviews effective
- In-person focus groups valuable
- Social media polls engage well

**Trust Building:**

- Personal connections matter
- Referrals open doors
- Patience in relationship building
- Respect for privacy

### Platform Insights

**Facebook:**

- Largest reach in Egypt
- All age groups present
- Groups are very active
- Video content performs well

**Instagram:**

- Growing rapidly
- Younger demographic
- Visual content focus
- Stories engagement high

**TikTok:**

- Exploding among youth
- Entertainment-focused
- Trend-driven
- High engagement rates

**WhatsApp:**

- Universal usage
