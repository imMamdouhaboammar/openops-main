## 🔍 Trend Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Relevance

**Question:** Does this fit our brand?

**Evaluation:**

- ✅ Aligns with brand values
- ✅ Relevant to our audience
- ✅ Fits our niche
- ✅ Authentic participation possible

**Score:** 1-10

---

### 2. Timing

**Question:** Are we early or late?

**Stages:**

- 🌱 **Emerging** (0-24h): High risk, high reward
- 🔥 **Peak** (1-3 days): Maximum visibility
- 📉 **Declining** (3-7 days): Lower impact
- 💀 **Dead** (7+ days): Avoid

**Optimal:** Emerging to Peak

**Score:** 1-10

---

### 3. Longevity

**Question:** Flash trend or sustained?

**Types:**

- **Flash:** 24-48 hours (act fast)
- **Short-term:** 1-2 weeks (plan quickly)
- **Sustained:** Months (strategic approach)
- **Evergreen:** Ongoing (always relevant)

**Strategy varies by type**

**Score:** 1-10

---

### 4. Risk Assessment

**Question:** Any controversy or danger?

**Red Flags:**

- Political content
- Religious sensitivity
- Controversial figures
- Divisive topics
- Offensive elements

**Decision:** If ANY red flags → Skip

**Score:** 1-10 (10 = safe, 1 = risky)

---

### 5. Opportunity

**Question:** Can we add unique value?

**Value-Add:**

- Unique perspective
- Educational angle
- Entertainment value
- Problem solution
- Brand differentiation

**If no value-add → Skip**

**Score:** 1-10

---

## 📋 Trend Participation Decision Matrix

**Minimum Scores to Participate:**

- Relevance: ≥7/10
- Timing: ≥6/10
- Risk: ≥8/10 (must be safe)
- Opportunity: ≥7/10

**Total:** ≥28/40

**If below threshold → Don't participate**

---

## ⚡ Trend Participation Strategy

### Quick Response (24-48 hours)

**For Flash Trends:**

**Hour 1-4:** Evaluate

- Is it relevant?
- Is it safe?
- Can we execute quickly?

**Hour 4-12:** Create

- Simple execution
- Use existing assets
- Quick production

**Hour 12-24:** Post

- Optimal timing
- Platform-appropriate
- Monitor response

---

### Planned Response (1-7 days)

**For Short-term Trends:**

**Day 1:** Evaluate & Plan

- Full trend analysis
- Creative brainstorm
- Resource allocation

**Day 2-3:** Create

- Quality execution
- Brand-aligned
- Value-added

**Day 4-5:** Post & Amplify

- Strategic timing
- Cross-platform
- Paid boost if needed

---

### Strategic Response (Sustained Trends)

**For Long-term Trends:**

**Week 1:** Research

- Deep trend analysis
- Audience interest
- Competitive landscape

**Week 2-3:** Develop

- Comprehensive content
- Series planning
- Multi-format approach

**Week 4+:** Execute

- Sustained campaign
- Regular content
- Community engagement

## 🚨 Anti-Hallucination in Trend Analysis

**CRITICAL:** Never fabricate trends

❌ **Don't:**

- Invent trending topics
- Fake trend statistics
- Claim something is trending when it's not
- Fabricate trend data

✅ **Do:**

- Verify trends are real
- Check multiple sources
- Document trend evidence
- State if unable to verify real-time trends

**Limitation Acknowledgment:**
"I cannot verify real-time trends. Please check [platform] trending page for current trends."
