## 🎯 Micro-Segmentation Strategy

### Segment by Content Interaction

**Super Fans:**

- Engage with 80%+ of content
- Comment regularly
- Share frequently
- **Strategy:** Exclusive content, early access, community building

**Regular Engagers:**

- Engage with 40-80% of content
- Occasional comments
- Moderate sharing
- **Strategy:** Consistent value, engagement prompts

**Passive Followers:**

- Engage with <40% of content
- Rare comments
- Minimal sharing
- **Strategy:** Re-engagement campaigns, value-packed content

**At-Risk:**

- No engagement in 30+ days
- **Strategy:** Win-back campaigns, surveys

---

### Segment by Purchase Behavior

**VIP Customers:**

- 3+ purchases
- High AOV
- Frequent buyers
- **Strategy:** Loyalty rewards, exclusive offers, early access

**Repeat Customers:**

- 2 purchases
- Moderate AOV
- **Strategy:** Upsell, cross-sell, referral incentives

**One-Time Buyers:**

- 1 purchase
- **Strategy:** Re-engagement, product recommendations

**Cart Abandoners:**

- Added to cart, didn't purchase
- **Strategy:** Retargeting, discount offers, urgency

**Browsers:**

- Visit site, no purchase
- **Strategy:** Educational content, social proof, offers

---

### Segment by Content Needs

**Learners:**

- Seek educational content
- Save tutorials
- **Content:** How-to guides, tips, deep dives

**Entertainers:**

- Seek fun content
- Share memes
- **Content:** Humor, trends, behind-the-scenes

**Shoppers:**

- Seek product info
- Click links
- **Content:** Product showcases, reviews, offers

**Community Seekers:**

- Seek connection
- Comment frequently
- **Content:** Q&A, polls, user-generated content
