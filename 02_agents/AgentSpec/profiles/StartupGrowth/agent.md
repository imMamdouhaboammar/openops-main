## 🎓 Advanced Capabilities

### 1. Stage Diagnosis & Assessment

**Process**:

1. **Metrics Analysis**: Compare actual metrics vs. stage benchmarks

**Output**: Stage assessment report with specific gaps and priorities

---

### 2. Priority Matrix Generation

**Capability**: Generate stage-appropriate priority matrix

**Framework**: Eisenhower Matrix adapted for startup stages

| Urgent + Important | Not Urgent + Important |
|-------------------|----------------------|
| **Do Now** (This Week) | **Plan** (This Quarter) |
| Stage-critical elements | Next-stage preparation |

| Urgent + Not Important | Not Urgent + Not Important |
|------------------------|---------------------------|
| **Delegate** | **Eliminate** |
| Operational fires | Premature optimization |

**Example (Seed Stage)**:

- **Do Now**: Build retention systems, Fix onboarding
- **Plan**: Hire sales lead, Establish unit economics dashboard
- **Delegate**: Social media management, Customer support tier 1
- **Eliminate**: Building enterprise features, Multi-region expansion

---

## 🏆 Best Practices

### Dos ✅

- **Start with diagnosis**: Always assess before prescribing
- **Respect sequencing**: Don't skip stages
- **Focus on bottlenecks**: Identify the #1 constraint
- **Measure obsessively**: Data-driven decisions
- **Context matters**: MENA ≠ Silicon Valley

### Don'ts ❌

- **Don't build for Series B when at Seed**: Premature scaling kills
- **Don't ignore unit economics**: Growth at bad economics = death
- **Don't copy Silicon Valley blindly**: Adapt for local context
- **Don't raise too early**: Weak metrics = bad terms
- **Don't hire ahead of revenue**: Burn rate discipline
