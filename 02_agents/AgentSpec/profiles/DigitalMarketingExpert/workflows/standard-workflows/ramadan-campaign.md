# Ramadan Campaign Launch Workflow (30-Day Sprint)

**Workflow ID**: `ramadan-campaign-launch`  
**Category**: Standard  
**Frequency**: Annual  
**Duration**: 8 weeks (4 planning + 4 execution)  
**Owner**: Digital Marketing Team

---

## Overview

Ramadan is the **most critical marketing period** in Saudi/GCC (30 days of fasting, iftar gatherings, charity, family time, and heightened digital consumption post-Iftar). This workflow ensures culturally-appropriate, high-impact campaigns.

### Objectives

1. **Brand Visibility**: Dominate Ramadan share of voice (SOV)
2. **Engagement**: 2-3x normal engagement rates (Ramadan effect)
3. **Sales Conversion**: Capitalize on Ramadan shopping (gifts, food, electronics)
4. **Cultural Resonance**: Authentic, respectful, values-aligned messaging

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Ramadan Planning (Weeks 1-4)

### Week 1-2: Strategy & Creative Concept

**Market Research**:

- **Competitor Analysis**: What did top brands do last Ramadan?
- **Trend Forecasting**: Hijri calendar dates, expected mood/themes
- **Audience Insights**: What resonates? (Family, charity, spirituality, togetherness)

**Creative Concept Development**:

- **Theme**: "Ramadan Kareem" messaging (generosity, blessing, unity)
- **Storytelling**: Emotional narratives (family reunion, charity impact, community)
- **Visual Identity**: Crescent moon, lanterns (فانوس), geometric patterns, warm colors (gold, purple, green)

**Content Pillars**:

1. **Spiritual**: Islamic values, Quran recitation, prayer reminders
2. **Family**: Iftar gatherings, family activities, recipes
3. **Charity**: Zakat, sadaqah, community giving
4. **Entertainment**: Ramadan series (مسلسلات رمضان), quizzes, contests

### Week 3: Influencer & Partnership Planning

**Influencer Selection (Saudi/GCC)**:

- **Tier Mix**: 1-2 macro (reach) + 5-10 micro (engagement) + 20 nano (authenticity)
- **Content Types**: Iftar recipes, charity campaigns, product unboxing, Ramadan vlogs
- **Compensation**: Mix of cash + product gifting + charity donations (in brand's name)

**Partnerships**:

- **Charities**: Co-branded Zakat campaigns (donate % of sales)
- **Food Brands**: Iftar meal bundles (restaurants, grocery)
- **Entertainment**: Sponsor Ramadan TV series, podcasts

### Week 4: Production & Asset Creation

**Content Production**:

- **Video Assets**: 15-30 second Ramadan greeting, product showcase, customer testimonials
- **Social Media Graphics**: Instagram feed templates, Snapchat filters, TikTok thumbnails
- **Arabic Copy**: Ramadan Kareem wishes, product descriptions, CTAs

**Landing Pages**:

- **Ramadan-Themed Design**: Crescent moon header, warm colors
- **Promotions**: Bundle deals, free shipping, Zakat calculator
- **SEO**: Arabic keywords ("عروض رمضان", "إفطار صحي", "هدايا عيد")

---

## Phase 2: Ramadan Execution (Weeks 5-8 = 30 Days)

### Week 1 of Ramadan: Launch & Awareness

**Day 1 (First Day of Ramadan)**:

- **Post**: "Ramadan Kareem!" greeting across all platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Snapchat, WhatsApp)
- **Ad Launch**: Google, Meta, TikTok, Snapchat campaigns go live
- **Influencer Activations**: First wave posts (iftar recipes, product features)

**Week 1 Focus**:

- **Brand Awareness**: High reach ads, video views
- **Community Engagement**: Daily Ramadan tips, quizzes, polls
- **Hashtag**: Create branded hashtag (#رمضان_مع_[Brand])

### Week 2-3: Engagement & Social Commerce

**Content Cadence**:

- **Daily Posts** (2-3x/day):
  - Morning (8-10 AM): Motivational/spiritual content
  - Afternoon (2-4 PM): Product showcases, tutorials
  - Evening (8-10 PM post-Iftar): Entertainment, quizzes, live sessions

**Live Shopping Events**:

- **WhatsApp**: Broadcast exclusive Ramadan deals to subscribers
- **Instagram Live**: Iftar meal prep with influencer, shoppable products
- **TikTok Live**: Q&A sessions, product giveaways

**User-Generated Content (UGC)**:

- **Campaign**: "Share Your Iftar" - customers post iftar photos with product, tag brand
- **Incentive**: Best photo wins gift card, featured on brand page
- **Hashtag**: #إفطاري_مع_[Brand]

### Week 4 (Last 10 Days + Eid Prep)

**Last 10 Days (Most Sacred)**:

- **Shift Tone**: More spiritual, less promotional
- **Charity Focus**: Highlight Zakat donations, charity partnerships
- **Content**: Quranic verses, night prayer reminders, Laylat Al-Qadr (Night of Power)

**Eid Preparation (Days 28-30)**:

- **Eid Greetings**: "Eid Mubarak" creative ready for Day 30 sunset (Eid announcement)
- **Final Sales Push**: "Last chance for Ramadan deals"
- **Eid Collection Launch**: Tease Eid products (clothing, gifts, sweets)

---

## Phase 3: Post-Ramadan (Eid & Analysis)

### Eid Al-Fitr (3-Day Holiday)

**Day 1 of Eid**:

- **Post**: "Eid Mubarak!" greeting at Fajr prayer time (morning)
- **Content**: Family celebration themes, gift-giving, joy
- **Promotions**: Eid sale (extend Ramadan deals OR launch new Eid offers)

**Days 2-3**:

- **UGC**: Encourage customers to share Eid celebrations
- **Thank You**: Acknowledge community engagement during Ramadan

### Campaign Analysis (Week After Eid)

**KPIs to Measure**:

- **Reach**: Total impressions, video views
- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares, saves (target: 2-3x baseline)
- **Conversion**: Sales revenue, ROAS, new customers acquired
- **Influencer Impact**: Engagement rate, referral traffic, promo code usage

**Learnings for Next Year**:

- **What Worked**: Top-performing content types, platforms, influencers
- **What Didn't**: Low-engagement posts, underperforming ads
- **Cultural Insights**: Audience feedback, sentiment analysis

## Cultural Do's & Don'ts

### ✅ DO

- Use "Ramadan Kareem" or "Ramadan Mubarak" greetings
- Show family, community, charity themes
- Respect prayer times (avoid posting during Maghrib/Isha)
- Highlight value of fasting, spirituality, togetherness
- Use warm, inviting visuals (gold, green, purple)

### ❌ DON'T

- Over-commercialize (balance sales with values)
- Show people eating/drinking during daytime (fasting hours)
- Use party/nightlife imagery (inappropriate for holy month)
- Ignore charity component (Zakat is pillar of Ramadan)
- Copy-paste global Ramadan campaigns (Saudi/GCC context matters)

## Success Metrics

### Campaign-Level

- ✅ **Reach**: ≥ 5M impressions across platforms
- ✅ **Engagement Rate**: ≥ 6% (vs. baseline 3%)
- ✅ **ROAS**: ≥ 5.0 (5 SAR revenue per 1 SAR ad spend)
- ✅ **New Customers**: ≥ 30% of sales from first-time buyers

### Brand-Level

- ✅ **Brand Sentiment**: ≥ 80% positive mentions (social listening)
- ✅ **Share of Voice**: Top 3 in category during Ramadan
- ✅ **Follower Growth**: ≥ 15% increase during month
