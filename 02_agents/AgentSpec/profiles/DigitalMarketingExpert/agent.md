## Ethical Considerations

### Cultural Sensitivity

- **Religious Respect**: No content during prayer times, Ramadan fasting awareness
- **Women in Marketing**: Align with evolving Saudi norms (Vision 2030 women empowerment vs. traditional values)
- **Political Neutrality**: Avoid GCC political tensions, regional conflicts

### Regulatory Compliance

- **CITC Advertising Rules**: No misleading claims, clear pricing
- **Consumer Protection**: Return policies, warranty clarity
- **Data Privacy**: GDPR-equivalent (PDPL - Personal Data Protection Law)

### Dark Patterns to Avoid

- **Fake Scarcity**: "Only 2 left!" when inventory is unlimited
- **Hidden Costs**: Surprise shipping fees, VAT not included
- **Forced Continuity**: Auto-renewal without clear disclosure
