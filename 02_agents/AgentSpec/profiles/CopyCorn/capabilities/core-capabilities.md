### Stage Deep Dives

#### Awareness Stage

**Metrics:**

- Reach and impressions
- Brand search volume
- Share of voice
- Aided/unaided awareness

**Levers:**

- Content marketing (SEO/social)
- Paid awareness campaigns
- PR and media
- Influencer partnerships

---

#### Acquisition Stage

**Metrics:**

- Traffic by source
- CAC by channel
- Lead quality scores
- Conversion rate (visitor → lead)

**Levers:**

- SEO (organic traffic)
- Paid acquisition (Google, Meta, etc.)
- Content distribution
- Partnership/affiliate

---

#### Activation Stage

**Metrics:**

- Signup completion rate
- Time to first value
- Onboarding completion %
- "Aha moment" achievement rate

**Diagnosis Questions:**

- What is the "aha moment"? (e.g., Slack: send 2000 messages as team)
- How long until users reach it?
- What % of signups reach it?

**Levers:**

- Onboarding optimization
- First-value acceleration
- Guided experiences
- Behavioral nudges

---

#### Retention Stage

**Metrics:**

- DAU/MAU ratio
- Cohort retention curves
- Churn rate
- Engagement frequency

**Levers:**

- Habit-forming features
- Email/push re-engagement
- Value reminders
- Community building

---

#### Revenue Stage

**Metrics:**

- Conversion rate (free → paid)
- ARPU/ARPA
- LTV
- Expansion revenue %

**Levers:**

- Pricing optimization
- Upsell/cross-sell
- Feature gating
- Value-based pricing

---

#### Referral Stage

**Metrics:**

- NPS score
- Referral rate
- Viral coefficient (K)
- Referral CAC vs. paid CAC

**Levers:**

- Referral program design
- Shareable moments
- Social proof systems
- Incentive structures

### Growth Loops Design

**Types of Loops:**

```
Create Content → SEO/Social → Traffic → Engagement → 
User Content → More Content → Loop
```

#### Referral Loop


## 4. Creative & Content Excellence

### Messaging Hierarchy

```
Level 1: Value Proposition (Why exist?)
    "We help [ICP] achieve [outcome] without [pain point]"
           ↓
Level 2: Key Messages (Supporting pillars)
    Message 1: [Benefit/feature cluster]
    Message 2: [Benefit/feature cluster]  
    Message 3: [Benefit/feature cluster]
           ↓
Level 3: Proof Points (Evidence)
    - Statistics
    - Testimonials
    - Case studies
    - Awards/recognition
           ↓
Level 4: Tactical Copy (Execution)
    Headlines, CTAs, body copy, social posts
```

---

### Hook Library

**Pain Hooks:**

- "Tired of [pain point]?"
- "Stop wasting time on [frustration]"
- "Why is [problem] so hard?"

**Desire Hooks:**

- "Imagine if you could [dream state]"
- "What would you do with [benefit]?"
- "Ready to [aspirational outcome]?"

**Curiosity Hooks:**

- "What if I told you [surprising truth]?"
- "The secret that [impressive group] use..."
- "Most people don't know this about [topic]..."

**Contrarian Hooks:**

- "Everyone thinks [common belief], but..."
- "We did the opposite and [result]"
- "Stop doing [popular thing]"

**Story Hooks:**

- "I used to [struggle], then..."
- "Three years ago, we almost [failure]..."
- "Here's what happened when [action]..."

**Social Proof Hooks:**

- "Join [X] companies who..."
- "[Number] customers can't be wrong"
- "Trusted by teams at [logos]"

### JTBD Framework

```
Jobs To Be Done Canvas:
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ FUNCTIONAL JOB                                          │

│ Example: "Manage my team's projects efficiently"        │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤

├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
```

## 5. Behavioral Economics Toolkit

### Persuasion Principles (Ethical Application)

#### Social Proof

```
Applications:
├── Numbers: "10,000+ companies use [product]"
├── Logos: "Trusted by Google, Nike, Spotify"
├── Testimonials: "Best decision we made" - CEO
├── Case studies: Detailed success stories
├── UGC: Real customer content
└── Activity: "50 people signed up today"
```

#### Scarcity (Ethical)

```
Ethical Applications:
├── Limited time: Real deadline-based offers
├── Limited quantity: Actual inventory limits
├── Exclusive access: Genuine early access
└── Avoid: Fake urgency, manipulative timers
```

#### Anchoring

```
Applications:
├── Price anchoring: Show original vs. discounted
├── Value anchoring: "Worth $500, get for $99"
├── Plan anchoring: Highlight "popular" tier
└── Comparison: "Cheaper than [alternative]"
```

#### Framing

```
Frame Types:
├── Loss vs. Gain: "Don't lose" > "You could gain"
├── Percentage vs. Absolute: Context-dependent
├── Present vs. Future: Immediate benefit emphasis
└── Positive vs. Negative: Outcome framing
```

#### Loss Aversion

```
Applications:
├── "Don't miss out on..."
├── Free trial expiration reminders
├── Sunk cost: "You've already invested..."
└── Risk reversal: Money-back guarantees
```
