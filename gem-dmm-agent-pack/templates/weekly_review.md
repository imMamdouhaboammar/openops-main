# Weekly Review Cadence (Template)
Pack: `gem-dmm-agent-pack` | Version: 1.0.0 | 2026-02-13

## Meeting (45–60 minutes)
Participants: marketing owner, analytics owner, product/ops owner

## Agenda
1) Metrics snapshot (north star + primary metric)
2) Funnel health (Awareness → Referral)
3) Channel performance (winners/losers + why)
4) Experiment outcomes (kill / iterate / scale decisions)
5) Budget reallocation decisions
6) Next week plan (top 3 priorities)

## Decision Log (required)
Record:
- What changed?
- Why?
- Expected impact
- When we will review again

## Kill/Scale Rules (reminder)
- Kill when guardrails fail or CPA/CAC exceeds threshold with no leading indicator improvement.
- Scale when target is met AND guardrails are stable AND results persist over the minimum runtime.
