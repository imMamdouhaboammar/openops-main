# Classified Intelligence Layer Spec

**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Date**: January 22, 2026  
**Scope**: 10_proprietary_intelligence

---

## What
Create a classified proprietary intelligence layer that gives the Marketing & Growth Agent an unfair but ethical advantage by combining signal fusion, creative genome modeling, demand engineering, competitive wargaming, and moat compounding.

## Why
Most marketing agents operate on best practices and surface-level analytics. This layer transforms the agent into a strategic intelligence system that predicts shifts, designs compounding advantage, and protects brand trust while scaling growth.

## Outcomes
- Signal fusion system with data lineage, reliability scoring, and privacy gates
- Creative genome engine that tags and recombines winning creative DNA
- Demand engineering playbook to create category-level pull, not only capture
- Competitive wargame protocol with pre-modeled counter-moves
- Moat compounding rules that convert wins into long-term advantage
- Behavioral archetype graph with trigger safeguards
- Offer resonance index to gate scaling decisions
- Attention yield curve for fatigue and marginal ROI control
- Trust signal ledger that enforces proof integrity
- Counterfactual growth simulator for baseline modeling
- Dark pattern firewall for ethical enforcement

## Constraints
- Must remain ethical and privacy-first
- Must disclose confidence levels and data source class
- Must be fully scripted (JSON/YML/MD/TXT)
- Must integrate with existing governance and experimentation rules

## Non-Goals
- Live data integrations
- Manipulative or deceptive tactics
- Replacement of existing Layer 10 systems
