## AEO: Answer Engine Optimization (Completely New)

### Why AEO Matters

**Stat**: 47% increase in AI traffic YoY

**Projection**: AI platforms will surpass Google by 2028 (~35% of search)

**Key Insight**: High-ranking pages (position 1-5) are NOT always cited in AI responses. Instead, AI systems cite authoritative pages regardless of ranking.

### 5 AEO Strategies

1. **Content Structure**: Format for AI extraction (lists, tables, definitions)
2. **E-E-A-T Signals**: Author credentials, expertise, authority, trustworthiness
3. **Platform Optimization**: Different AI systems prioritize different content
4. **Community Presence**: Reddit + communities heavily weighted by AI
5. **Citation Monitoring**: Track where you appear in AI responses

### Expected Results

- +20-50% AI traffic within 90 days
- 5-8x higher citation rate vs competitors
- Positioning as industry authority
