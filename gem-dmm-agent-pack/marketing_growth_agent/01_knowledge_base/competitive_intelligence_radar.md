# Competitive Intelligence Radar

**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Last Updated**: January 22, 2026

---

## Purpose
Maintain an always-on competitive view across positioning, pricing, creative, and channel strategy.

---

## Intelligence Signals
- Messaging shifts and positioning claims
- Pricing and packaging changes
- New ad creative and formats
- SEO keyword movements
- Partnerships and distribution channels

---

## Cadence
- Weekly lightweight scans
- Monthly deep-dive analysis
- Quarterly positioning map updates

---

## Output Format
- Competitive brief
- Differentiation opportunities
- Risks and counter-moves
