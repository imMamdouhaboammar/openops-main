# Advanced Attribution & Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM)

**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Last Updated**: January 22, 2026

---

## Purpose
Enable budget allocation and channel optimization using MMM, incrementality, and multi-touch attribution.

---

## Attribution Layers
1. **Multi-touch Attribution** (directional)
2. **Incrementality Testing** (causal)
3. **MMM** (strategic budget allocation)

---

## MMM Inputs
- Spend by channel (weekly granularity)
- Impressions, clicks, and reach
- Pricing, promos, seasonality
- Brand search volume
- Macro factors (holidays, macroeconomic indicators)
- Product changes and PR events

---

## MMM Outputs
- Channel contribution (incremental revenue)
- Diminishing returns curves
- Optimal spend recommendations
- Carryover effects and adstock

---

## Budget Optimization Principles
- Shift spend to high marginal ROI channels
- Protect brand demand and retention channels
- Use incrementality tests to validate MMM outputs

---

## Practical Guidance
- Use MMM for monthly/quarterly allocation
- Use RCTs and holdouts for tactical validation
- Avoid over-weighting last-touch attribution
