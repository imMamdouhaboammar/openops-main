# Marketing Channel Benchmarks & Feasibility Reference

This document provides reference benchmarks for feasibility validation. All figures are approximate ranges for 2025-2026 and should be validated against current market data.

---

## Paid Channels

### Google Search Ads

- **Typical cost**: $1-5K/month (SMB) to $50K+/month (enterprise)
- **CPC range**: $1-8 (B2C), $5-50+ (B2B, competitive terms)
- **Timeline to results**: 1-2 weeks to initial data, 4-6 weeks to optimize
- **Best for ICP**: High-intent searchers, any industry
- **Team requirement**: Campaign manager (part-time to full-time)
- **Minimum viable budget**: $1,000/month

### LinkedIn Ads

- **Typical cost**: $5-10K/month (B2B minimum viable)
- **CPC range**: $8-20 (sponsored content), $5-15 (message ads)
- **CPM range**: $30-80
- **Timeline to results**: 2-4 weeks initial, 6-8 weeks optimize
- **Best for ICP**: B2B, enterprise, professional roles (CTO, VP, Director)
- **Team requirement**: Campaign manager + content creator
- **Minimum viable budget**: $3,000/month (limited reach)

### Facebook/Instagram Ads

- **Typical cost**: $1-5K/month (SMB) to $20K+/month (scale)
- **CPC range**: $0.50-3 (B2C), $2-10 (B2B)
- **Timeline to results**: 1-3 weeks
- **Best for ICP**: B2C consumers, e-commerce, local businesses
- **NOT ideal for**: Enterprise B2B decision-makers
- **Team requirement**: Campaign manager + creative designer
- **Minimum viable budget**: $500/month

### TikTok Ads

- **Typical cost**: $3-10K/month
- **CPC range**: $0.50-2
- **Timeline to results**: 1-3 weeks
- **Best for ICP**: Gen Z/Millennial consumers, lifestyle brands, GCC markets
- **Team requirement**: Video content creator + campaign manager
- **Minimum viable budget**: $1,000/month

### Snapchat Ads

- **Typical cost**: $3-8K/month
- **Swipe-up CPC**: $1-3
- **Timeline to results**: 1-3 weeks
- **Best for ICP**: Young demographics, GCC markets (strong in KSA/UAE)
- **Team requirement**: Creative designer + campaign manager
- **Minimum viable budget**: $1,000/month

### Display/Retargeting

- **Typical cost**: $1-5K/month
- **CPM range**: $2-10
- **Timeline to results**: 2-4 weeks
- **Best for**: Retargeting existing visitors, brand awareness
- **Minimum viable budget**: $500/month

## Organic Channels

### SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

- **Typical cost**: $2-10K/month (agency) or team time
- **Timeline to results**: 6-12 months for significant results
- **Best for ICP**: Any high-intent search audience
- **Team requirement**: SEO specialist + content writer
- **NOT feasible when**: Budget < $1K/month or timeline < 3 months

### Content Marketing

- **Typical cost**: $2-8K/month (content production)
- **Timeline to results**: 3-6 months for traction, 6-12 months for compounding
- **Best for ICP**: B2B thought leadership, complex purchase decisions
- **Team requirement**: Content strategist + writer + designer
- **NOT feasible when**: Timeline < 3 months

### Email Marketing / Lifecycle/CRM

- **Typical cost**: $500-3K/month (platform + creation)
- **Timeline to results**: 2-4 weeks (with existing list)
- **Best for ICP**: Existing subscribers/customers, retention
- **Team requirement**: Email marketer + copywriter
- **Prerequisite**: Existing email list

### Social Media Organic

- **Typical cost**: Team time + tools ($200-1K/month)
- **Timeline to results**: 3-6 months for meaningful engagement
- **Best for ICP**: Brand awareness, community building
- **Team requirement**: Social media manager
- **NOT feasible when**: Expecting direct leads from organic social

## Partnership & Direct Channels

### Influencer Marketing

- **Typical cost**: $1-20K/campaign (micro to macro)
- **Timeline to results**: 2-6 weeks per campaign
- **Best for ICP**: B2C, lifestyle, GCC markets
- **Team requirement**: Partnership manager

### Events/Webinars

- **Typical cost**: $2-10K/event
- **Timeline to results**: 4-8 weeks (planning + execution)
- **Best for ICP**: B2B enterprise, high-ticket products
- **Team requirement**: Event coordinator + subject matter expert

### Account-Based Marketing (ABM)

- **Typical cost**: $5-20K/month
- **Timeline to results**: 3-6 months
- **Best for ICP**: Enterprise accounts, named account lists
- **Team requirement**: ABM specialist + SDR + content creator
- **Minimum viable budget**: $5,000/month

### Outbound Sales / Cold Outreach

- **Typical cost**: $3-8K/month (SDR salary/tools)
- **Timeline to results**: 4-8 weeks
- **Best for ICP**: B2B, defined target list
- **Team requirement**: Full-time SDR

## Regional Considerations (GCC/MENA)

### Platform Preferences

- **Egypt**: Facebook/Instagram dominant, WhatsApp for CRM
- **KSA**: Snapchat & TikTok strong, Twitter/X active, LinkedIn for B2B
- **UAE**: Instagram & LinkedIn strong, TikTok growing, Google dominant for search
- **General GCC**: WhatsApp as core CRM/retention surface

### Seasonal Demand Shifts

- **Ramadan/Eid**: Major shopping season, adjust ad spend and messaging
- **Hajj/Umrah season**: Consider Saudi-specific demand patterns
- **Summer**: Travel and lifestyle budgets shift
- **Back-to-school**: August-September surge in education/retail
- **National holidays**: Saudi National Day (Sep 23), UAE National Day (Dec 2), Egypt Revolution Day (Jul 23)

### Cultural Considerations

- Relationship-driven decisions (longer sales cycles)
- Reputation and authority sensitivity
- Committee/group decision-making common
- Risk-averse buyers in enterprise segment
