

# Facebook Content Tactics
Version: 1.0
Region Context: Egypt, GCC, MENA
Applies To: B2B, B2C, D2C, Enterprise, Regulated Markets

---

## Purpose

This document defines tactical **content strategies for Facebook** that help brands build:

- Awareness
- Consideration
- Engagement
- Intent progression
- Trust
- Conversion support
- Retention

All tactics are designed to align with the buyer journey stages and measurable signals.

---

## 1. Core Content Tactics

These strategies focus on *purposeful content* — not random posting.

---

### 1.1 Educational & Insightful Posts

**Overview:**  
Create posts that teach, explain, or simplify complex industry topics.

**Examples:**  
- “3 common mistakes in [industry] + how to avoid them”  
- Infographics summarizing key data  
- Step-by-step explanation threads

**Use When:**  
Consideration → Active Evaluation

**Why:**  
Audience seeks solutions — this establishes credibility and perceived expertise.

---

### 1.2 Storytelling & Social Proof

**Overview:**  
Use narratives that build relatability and demonstrate real results.

**Examples:**  
- Short case summaries  
- Customer testimonials (text + photo/video)  
- Project spotlight stories

**Use When:**  
Evaluation → Trust

**Why:**  
Social proof reduces perceived risk and builds confidence in decision makers.

---

### 1.3 Visual & Video-First Formats

**Overview:**  
Prioritize video content and strong visuals to increase engagement and reach.

**Examples:**  
- Short educational videos  
- Mini explainers  
- Visual slides summarizing insights

**Use When:**  
All stages, especially when interactivity is low

**Why:**  
Facebook favors engaging media; video increases watch-time and algorithmic reach.

---

### 1.4 Live Sessions & Q&A

**Overview:**  
Host interactive sessions addressing audience questions and industry themes.

**Examples:**  
- Expert Q&A session  
- Live demo walk-through  
- Panel discussion with partners

**Use When:**  
Consideration → Engagement

**Why:**  
Direct interaction increases trust and signals strong intent.

---

### 1.5 Audience Polls & Interactive Questions

**Overview:**  
Use polls and question posts to prompt participation.

**Examples:**  
- “Which challenge matters most to you?”  
- “Choose the top priority for 2026”

**Use When:**  
Awareness → Consideration

**Why:**  
Engagement increases content visibility and gives insight into audience needs.

---

## 2. Content Examples

Examples of quick, actionable post formats:

- **Tip Carousel Post:** “5 Steps to Reduce [Risk] in Your Team”
- **Visual Chart Post:** Data insights for industry benchmarking
- **Mini Video:** 60-second explanation of a key concept
- **Client Story:** “Before → After” outcome snapshot
- **Poll Post:** “Select biggest business challenge”

---

## 3. Content Principles

Use these guardrails for quality and consistency:

- **Provide Value First:** Avoid hard selling; educate and inform  
- **Keep It Local:** Tailor cultural and regional context for Egypt, GCC, MENA  
- **Maintain Brand Voice:** Consistent tone and narrative  
- **Use Clear CTAs:** Always hint next-step (Download, Comment, Share)  
- **Visual First Order:** Use visuals and video for higher engagement

---

## 4. Deployment Logic

Use the following as tactical triggers to decide **when to publish what**:

| Journey Stage | Tactic | Signal |
|---------------|--------|--------|
| Awareness | Educational Posts, Polls | Reach + Follows |
| Consideration | Visual Videos, Live Q&A | Watch Time + Comments |
| Evaluation | Social Proof Stories | Saves + Shares |
| Trust | Testimonials | Clicks to Profile + Inquiries |
| Conversion Support | Case Summaries | Clicks to Website |
| Retention | Community Posts | Repeat Interactions |

---

## 5. Creative Guidelines

Follow these rules for production:

- **Post Length:** Prefer short, concise posts  
- **Video Duration:** 30–90 seconds optimal  
- **Captions:** Use descriptive text with key takeaways  
- **Design Consistency:** Branded colors + typography  
- **Localization:** Arabic + English when relevant

---

## 6. Signal-Driven Content Metrics

Measure based on engagement that signals intent:

- **Reach & Unique Views** – Awareness  
- **Engaged View Time** – Consideration  
- **Post Saves & Shares** – Evaluation  
- **CTA Clicks (Download, Contact)** – Trust/Conversion  
- **Repeat Visits** – Retention

## 8. Regional Adaptation Rules

Content must be:

- **Translated or localized** depending on audience  
- **Culturally sensitive and relevant**  
- **Referencing regional examples or data**  
- **Aligned with local narratives of trust and authority**

This increases resonance and reduces friction.

---

## 9. Integration with Facebook Ads

Organic content signals help inform **which ads to amplify**:

- High-engagement videos → Boost as Ads  
- Poll winners → Use in custom audiences  
- Case stories → Repurpose for lead generation creative

Organic content informs **paid focus**, not vice versa.

---

## 10. Completion Condition

Facebook Content Strategy is complete when:

- Each decision stage has defined tactics  
- Each tactic is measurable and tied to intent  
- Content aligns with brand real need  
- Deployment logic matches buyer psychology
