# Strategy Generator Prompt Kit
Pack: `gem-dmm-agent-pack` | Version: 1.0.0 | 2026-02-13

Use this guide to generate a full strategy AFTER:
1) Discovery interview is complete, AND
2) CQ is valid and locked, AND
3) Grounding plan is created (and executed if browsing enabled).

---

## Required Strategy Spine (apply to every section)
Use:
**If [Truth] then [Direction] by [Action].**

Rules:
- “Truth” must be evidence-backed or explicitly labeled as **Assumption/Hypothesis**.
- Actions must be testable within 7–14 days where possible.
- Tie each action to a metric and a failure mode.

---

## Required Funnel Diagnostics
Cover end-to-end:
1) Awareness
2) Activation
3) Retention
4) Revenue
5) Referral

For each stage:
- current baseline (if known)
- top constraint
- biggest leak
- top 1–2 high-leverage fixes
- measurement needed

---

## Strategy Output Structure (must include all domains)

### 0) Executive Summary (decision-ready)
- CQ (one sentence)
- 3–5 strategic bets (no tactics yet)
- 30/60/90 day outcomes (ranges, not promises)

### 1) Positioning & Category
- Category narrative: what market you’re in and why it matters now
- Differentiation: why you win vs alternatives
- Message pillars and proof points

### 2) ICP & Segmentation
- Primary ICP + 1–2 secondary segments
- Buying triggers and objections
- Segmentation for targeting and CRM

### 2.5) Regional Context & Localization (Egypt + GCC)
- Market-specific nuances (Egypt vs KSA vs UAE vs GCC)
- Cultural and religious sensitivity notes
- Language/dialect guidance for customer-facing copy
- Seasonality windows (Ramadan/Eid, Hajj/Umrah, back-to-school, national holidays)
- Local payment and purchase behaviors (if relevant)

### 3) Offer & Messaging
- Offer structure (trial, guarantee, bundle, pricing)
- Pricing/packaging hypotheses (if relevant)
- Message testing plan

### 4) Channel Mix (fit + economics + constraints)
For each channel:
- why it fits the ICP and funnel stage
- budget/time expectation
- success metrics
- key risks and mitigations

### 5) Paid Media (testing matrix)
Build a testing matrix:
- audiences: core, lookalike, retargeting, intent, competitor
- creatives: 3–5 angles, formats, hooks
- landing variants: 2–3
- bidding notes: objective choice, learning constraints, frequency caps

### 6) SEO (intent map + clusters + technical priorities)
- Intent map: TOFU/MOFU/BOFU terms and pages
- Topic clusters and internal linking plan
- Technical priorities (indexation, CWV, structured data)
- Content briefs outline

### 7) Content System (pillars → formats → cadence)
- 3–5 pillars
- formats: video, short, long, email, case studies, tools
- cadence (weekly schedule)
- repurposing map (1 piece → many outputs)

### 8) Social (organic + community + distribution)
- platforms and rationale
- content hooks and proof strategy
- community loops (if applicable)

### 9) Lifecycle/CRM
- onboarding messages
- activation nudges
- retention sequences
- winback
- segmentation and personalization rules

### 10) Partnerships
- partner types
- outreach script concept
- co-marketing assets

### 11) CRO
- friction audit plan
- proof strategy (testimonials, case studies)
- offer clarity improvements
- speed/UX basics checklist

### 12) Analytics/Tracking
- GA4 + GTM event taxonomy
- conversion definitions
- attribution notes and limitations
- data quality checks

### 13) Experimentation Program
For each experiment:
- hypothesis
- variants
- primary metric + guardrails
- duration and decision rules (kill/iterate/scale)

### 14) Budget Split
- propose split by channel and stage
- explain assumptions
- define reallocation rules based on results

### 15) Creative System
- creative pipeline
- fatigue detection heuristics
- iteration cadence

### 16) Ops Cadence
- weekly review meeting agenda
- dashboard review
- decision log
- next sprint plan

## Per-Section Required Blocks (hard requirement)
For every major section include:
1) **Assumptions**
2) **Evidence Citations Plan** (placeholders ok until browsing is done)
3) **Evidence Confidence Score (0..1)** + short justification
4) **Next Step Questions** (only if needed; max 5)
